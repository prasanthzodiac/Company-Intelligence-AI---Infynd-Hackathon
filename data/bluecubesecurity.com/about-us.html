Blue Cube Security

Protecting and Guiding in a Digital World

Blue Cube Security are one of the UK’s largest independent Information and Cyber Security solution providers. Leading with a consultative approach, Blue Cube Security has been providing expertise and agile services to its customers for over 25 years. Operating from a UK head office working nationally.

Blue Cube Security take a customer focused approach and prides itself on being agile, giving them the ability to work with customers as a partnership. The team understand the importance of providing the right solution that integrates existing policies, technologies and procedures, delivering confidence that valuable assets are secure and protected. The team holds valuable market knowledge enabling them to share expertise and guidance of current Information and cyber security issues, risks and solutions.

The team’s breadth of market knowledge and consultative approach apply their expertise to detect and assess, using Blue Cube Security’s methodology of ‘Intelligent Protection’ to help identify what, how and where to protect valuable assets.

Every team member attends the Blue Cube Security Academy to build a foundation of solid knowledge of the market through a comprehensive, continuous training programme, delivered alongside our key technology partners.

The Cynergy team provide flexible and scalable professional and managed security services to protect customers within the ever-increasing complexity of the cyber security threat landscape. The dedicated team are on hand to advise and consult on a range of technical solutions giving customers the opportunity to reduce operational costs and increase the effectiveness of their Information and cyber security solutions. Blue Cube Security prides itself on being highly accredited across a range of best of breed solutions in the Information and cyber security market, as well as having certifications in major governance and compliance frameworks.

Blue Cube Security works with its customers from initial consultation through to solution implementation, optimisation and education. The company’s best practice is to offer exceptional customer service, supporting customers throughout a project and after with a UK based 24x7 support desk, giving customers continued peace of mind when they need it most.

Meet the Leadership Team

CEO & FOUNDER

Gary Haycock-West

Gary's passion for work started out over 40 years ago in IT operations at IBM where he quickly started to understand the pain points of the customers via his support experience, this set him up for his journey into sales.

His experience has been the motivation for founding, growing and investing in business across many verticals over the years extending beyond the UK and into Europe.

Gary was awarded Deloitte and Touche 6 top growing company in the UK for a services business and has a proven entrepreneurial track record with the ability to identify and leverage the strengths at both business and team levels to build performance and bottom-line benefits.

Gary started Blue Cube Security in 2000 after identifying that the cyber threat landscape was on the increase. He applied the skills he had learnt from his IT career to build the company which is now one of the country's longest standing providers of independent advice for IT and cyber security solutions.

Gary is very well recognised and respected within his industries and shares his expertise to help other companies succeed. As a Business Coach, he is currently a Non-Executive Director for a mixture of start-ups, mature companies, and local and national charities to help identify positive change. A retired rugby player, he also shares his expertise as Vice President for both East Grinstead RFC and Guernsey RFC, his local rugby club.

DIRECTOR

Rob Swainson

Rob has over 20 years’ experience within the cyber security market. He began his career with Blue Cube Security in 2003, an organisation which he believed truly embodied his personal ethos of honest, independent and tailor-made IT and cyber security solutions.

He has a keen interest in all elements of the sales lifecycle, often heading up campaigns as well as ensuring that all the team receive constant training on products, industry trends and knowledge to maintain Blue Cube Security’s position at the forefront of the industry.

Rob is passionate about building relationships. Rob’s reputation, experience and expertise in the cyber security market has helped secure Blue Cube Security position as one of the UK’s leading provider of independent IT and Cyber security solutions.

TECHNOLOGY SERVICES DIRECTOR

Nick Munro

Nick Munro’s wealth of experience is underpinned by his bank of qualifications and chartered professional status. Nick joined Blue Cube Security in 2001 and his talent lies in translating ideas into technically sound and commercially beneficial results.

Nick understands the need for an organisation’s IT security solutions’ requirements to be evaluated against their existing specific infrastructure and operating environment whilst taking into account their business aspirations.

SALES & MARKETING DIRECTOR

Nick Kellaway

Nick has a proven track record across a number of sales and business development management positions in distributor, reseller, and vendor roles. The breadth of this experience has given him a unique insight into the cyber security channel.

Attracted by Blue Cube Security’s reputation as a leading independent cyber security provider with a first-class technical skillset, Nick thrives on his wide-ranging responsibilities and is passionate about mentoring his team, helping them to succeed and grow as individuals. With his strong work ethic, extensive business acumen and proactive and collaborative approach, Nick leads by example.
