Cynergy Cyber Security Services

Professional and Managed Security Services

 

It is essential that organisations protect their network and assets adequately and when the correct IT security infrastructure is in place, an organisation will flourish, seeing business growth and value-added benefits. Blue Cube Security enables organisations to gain this important business advantage, reducing risk and mitigating vulnerabilities and threats through sound practices, applied security technology and a continuous improvement framework. The Cynergy team are accredited IT professionals who will design, develop, implement, manage and support solutions that meet your business requirements.

CYNERGY GOVERNANCE – Many companies these days need to attain a level of certificated or accredited documented cyber security. Cynergy Governance is designed to take your project from inception through to accreditation by our selected partners.

CYNERGY INTELLIGENCE – Our initial engagement service is comprised of a Cyber Security Posture Review (Health Check) and may be supplemented with both initial and ongoing penetration tests on the infrastructure or customer applications.

CYNERGY EDUCATE – More than 90% of cyber security incidents have a human component. Our Cynergy Educate service has been designed to provide and maintain security awareness using the latest technology and artificial intelligence. Most importantly, our Cynergy Educate service seeks to grow your team’s security awareness to ensure they are an additional line of defence. 

CYNERGY SAFESCAN – Our vulnerability scanning service covers internal and external IP’s and web services. The frequency of our Cynergy SafeScan service can be set to suit your needs and can also be timed around the your patching regime. The service has various levels from a basic automated service to full reporting at agreed times.

CYNERGY PERIMETER – A perimeter firewall is often your first line of defence. Firewall management for both on-prem and cloud-base infrastructure is absolutely critical and Cynergy Perimeter is designed to provide and maintain the highest level of security utilising solutions from some of the leading firewall vendors.

CYNERGY ENDPOINT – Securing the endpoints on your network, whether they are servers, PCs, Macs or other IoT devices, is crucial.  Our Cynergy Endpoint service is designed to maintain your endpoint security at the highest level and we are proud to offer solutions utilising Trend Micro technology.

CYNERGY CLOUD SECURE – With increased adoption of cloud functionality and infrastructure and the fact that the provider secures the cloud, but the end user must secure their components, we have developed our skill base within our Cynergy Cloud Secure service to offer some of the best advice and design to ensure your cloud-based operations are secure.

CYNERGY REMOTE – Organisations often have a few offices with external IP’s and with the increased migration of staff to home or local working has meant the infrastructure has had to adapt. At the same time cyber security elements need to be adapted or redesigned to suit. Our Cynergy Remote service reviews the customer world and offers advice, design and appropriate services to ensure the highest levels of security are maintained for staff who are remote working often using non-customer equipment.

CYNERGY REMEDIATE – The skill set of the hacking community is rapidly growing in capability, and even with some of the best defensive strategies an incident may occur. Our Cynergy Remediate team is available either via contract or on request to provide remediation services to restore customer confidence in their infrastructure or security. 

CYNERGY MDR – By combining some of the services already mentioned and offering them on either a 24/7 or 16/7(out of hours) basis alongside a managed service to detect and respond to threats, Cynergy MDR adds another layer of responsiveness and security awareness to your cyber security posture.

CYNERGY ADVISOR – Many companies have a retained security officer at a senior level in their organisation, a CISO (Chief Information Security Officer). Whilst CISOs can use  our Cynergy Advisor  service to help shape current strategy, design and cyber security architecture, smaller companies and those without a CISO can use the service to ensure they are applying the most appropriate cyber security strategies. The service assists in the design phases allowing you to go-to-market with a design and strategy that best protects your business.
