Is Your Business Nobody Else’s Business?

Watch our webinar in collaboration with Cyber Risk Aware where we discuss how you need a human firewall as your last line of defence.

The old-school awareness training approach just doesn’t hack it anymore. With 7-10% of malicious emails making it through your email filters, it’s equally as important to ensure users are continually educated using security awareness training. By doing so, you will improve your organisation’s security posture, and keep users from participating in the spread of malicious emails.

See how easy it is to train and phish your users: 

  • Train your users with access to the vast library of awareness training content.
  • Send fully automated simulated phishing attacks, including of customisable templates with unlimited usage.
  • Brandable Content feature.
  • Assessments allow you to find out where your users are in both security knowledge and security culture to help establish baseline security metrics you can improve over time.
  • Risk and Compliance Reporting
