Freespace IQ – Workstation Occupancy.

Average Peak Occupancy (Tuesday to Thursday)

Americas.

Across the Americas, workstation occupancy averages 36% during mid-week peaks, highlighting lower desk use and wide variation between offices.

EMEA.

In Europe, the Middle East, and Africa, workstation utilization sits at 45% on core weekdays, with strong performers balanced by sites with far lower use.

APAC.

For Asia-Pacific, average workstation occupancy reaches 48% during mid-week peaks, though results vary significantly despite some offices operating at high levels.

*Visual representation only. Data accurate as of November 2025. For most recent data, please submit your details to download the latest report.

Your questions answered.

The Freespace IQ Global Workplace Insights Report is a quarterly research publication that combines Freespace’s proprietary occupancy and booking data with trusted industry benchmarks.

It provides insights into global workplace trends, hybrid work patterns, space utilization, and data-driven design recommendations for real estate and workplace leaders.

The report is designed for corporate real estate leaders, workplace strategists, designers, and anyone interested in understanding how offices are evolving.

It helps organizations benchmark their performance, make informed space decisions, and plan workplace strategies based on data.

The report draws on anonymized insights from over 185,000 managed office spaces and more than 8 million annual bookings.

It includes occupancy rates, meeting room and desk usage, behavioral patterns, and trends at city, regional, and global levels, supplemented with industry research for comparison.

The report is released quarterly, giving readers a regular, up-to-date view of evolving workplace trends.
