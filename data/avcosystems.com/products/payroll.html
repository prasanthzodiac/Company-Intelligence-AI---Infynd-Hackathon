Our Services

Avco’s Payroll Bureau service allows your business to eliminate the difficulties involved in payroll filing

Flexible transfer

Files can be sent to us via email or SFTP. For optimal security we advise sending them through our SFX system on the web.

Fully Automated

Fully automated and seamless data exchange, including data validation where necessary

Safe & Secure

We only keep your data until it is accepted by HM Revenue & Customs (HMRC) and while it is with us we keep it encrypted.

Format conversion

We offer a format conversion service with custom-built interfacing based on a range of ’near-the-shelf’ modules tailored to meet your specific needs

Compression & IRmark

We’ve automated the difficult process of conforming to HM Revenue & Customs’ (HMRC) data compression formats & IRmark specification so you don’t have to worry about it

Customer Support

With over 10 years of experience in handling payroll, our team is well placed to offer excellent customer support and guidance

What we do for GDPR >

  • Payroll Bureau

Avco Payroll Bureau service helps with file conversion and transfers, whether for year-end returns (P11D, P46CAR) or for file exchanges with HMRC throughout the year

The service includes overseeing the conversion, transfer and delivery of files. If your payroll software does not currently accept or produce HMRC data in the specified XML format, we offer a format conversion service with custom-built interfacing based on a range of 'near-the-shelf' modules tailored to meet your specific needs. This allows you to retain your existing payroll system, but still comply with new Inland Revenue requirements.

Files can be sent to us via email or SFTP. For optimal security we advise sending them through our SFX system on the web. We then handle the processing and compiling, allowing you to focus more on intricate aspects of your business.

Avco Payroll EDI (Electronic Data Interchange) enables the electronic transfer of payroll form files to and from HMRC

Avco Payroll EDI enables the electronic transfer of payroll form files to and from HMRC. The process provides a simple and secure route for all your standard submissions to and from your payroll system.

By managing file transfers from end-to-end Payroll EDI:

  • Protects sensitive data
  • Automates file management
  • Tracks files for corporate governance and compliance
