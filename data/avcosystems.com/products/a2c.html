    For Awarding Organisations

    Avco’s data transport service is the market leader in the secure transfer of data between Awarding Organisations and centres. It takes care of the management of centre access keys, embargoed uploads, result splitting, and supports multiple integration options. All while maintaining an impressive level of performance and throughput.

    The majority of JCQ awarding organisations use our transport service, and it has proven time and again to provide rock-solid reliability on GCSE and A-Level result days.

    For MIS

    Avco A2MIS offers a streamlined, easy-to-integrate REST API that enables seamless communication with awarding organisations. Use A2MIS to seamlessly integrate the submission of entries and retrieval of results directly within your MIS - without the complexity of JCQ Data Transport protocols or requiring the JCQ Data Transport application.

    For Local Authorities

    AnyComms Plus is a web-based secure file transfer system that is powerful and flexible. It keeps things simple for users whilst still providing a wide array of advanced features and security controls.
