Our Services

  • Computer Forensics
  • Mobile & Tablet Forensics
  • Cell Site Forensics
  • Vehicle Forensics
  • Drone Forensics

Working with both server infrastructure and end-user devices, we can quickly establish where to find the information we need. We will report this back to you in line with your requirements, in time and on budget.

We work across all areas requiring digital forensics, including:

- Intelligence gathering.

- Law enforcement.

- Industrial applications.

As the computer people entrust most of their information to, smartphones are often a rich source of evidence. We use the latest tools to discover information which users often have no idea is stored on the device. Even damaged devices can yield valuable information when we apply our smart processes to them.

We don't work with a single toolset or software vendor, ensuring that we have a wide range of options for the task in hand, whether that is iOS, Android, or another mobile OS.

Cell site analysis is an area where our expertise can add huge value. The combination of the latest equipment, software tools and bespoke code is what can make the difference between a successful outcome and an unsuccessful one.

More than just a history of who has been where, and when, analysis of satnav data allows us to analyse behaviour and spot patterns - and exceptions. Combined with other data sources, this can yield valuable insight into people's activities, broadening the evidential picture.

For example, when a person syncs their contacts with a car, they introduce a rich seam of data which we can mine later, cross-referencing calls made and received with locations visited.

Modern drones contain reams of data, from the logging of full GPS history to still and video image data which may be present on the drone itself. Even if a user manually sets the 'home point' on a drone to something different from its take-off location, the GPS history can form a valuable trail to prove when and where it has been used.

Combined with other services, such as cell site forensics, this can help us to establish exactly where a person was, and what they were doing, to a high evidential standard.
