General Cyber and Information Security Training

Protect your information

Keeping your information out of the wrong hands can be more challenging than it seems. Our General Cyber Security Course sets you up with the skills you and your organisation need to be able to protect yourselves against online intruders. 

This course is for anyone who uses the Internet a significant amount in their working life. Our aim is to help everyone reach a point where they can take personal responsibility for keeping their information secure. Furthermore, the course covers what to do in the unfortunate event that confidential information is taken and potentially exposed. 

You will need Internet access to partake in this course. No prior knowledge is required. 

It is suitable for all levels of expertise.

"No prior knowledge is required."

These courses cover key topics, such as:

- How to spot phishing attacks

- How to identify potential security incidents

- How to treat information properly

All courses can be tailored to an organisation’s specific security policies. They are aimed at any and every member of staff within an organisation, to ensure that everyone understands their responsibilities when it comes to information security. This will go a long way towards protecting the information of yourself and your clients.

- Interactive activities.

- Real-life case studies.

- Written text.

- Online, to be done at your own pace, with no time limits on assignments.

- Developed by professionals.

- CPD accredited.

- Equipped with audio voiceover.

- Approx. 2 hours in duration.

Furthermore, on completion, a certificate will be posted to you the next working day.
