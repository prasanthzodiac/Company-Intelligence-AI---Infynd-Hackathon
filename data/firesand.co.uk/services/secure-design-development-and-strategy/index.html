Secure Design, Development and Strategy

Protection:

As with any form of defence, cyber security will quickly become ineffective if it isn't backed up by a clear vision of where resources most need to be focused, a plan to deliver them, and a roadmap to sustain them as the threats ranged against your cyber systems evolve. This is why you need Firesand. We're certified cyber security experts with decades of secure design, development and strategy experience across industries, vertical sectors and businesses of all sizes. 

Firesand can take you from initial security health check to a fully realised cyber security strategy. Through secure architectures for cloud environments, security solutions, and enterprise-wide deployments, we can protect your existing and evolving infrastructure from the ground up.

Cloud & Cyber Services

  • Secure Design, Development And Strategy Services
  • Cloud Security Architect Services
  • Security Solutions Architecture
  • Enterprise Security Architecture (ESA)

Firesand have experienced, trusted security advisers, architects and engineers who are happy to guide you through the process of translating business objectives into a strategic security roadmap.

This approach ensures that security is cost-effectively aligned to your business goals, whilst intelligently delivering an effective, comprehensive, future-ready risk management platform.

Firesand's cloud security architects define, design and implement secure cloud migrations and integrations with your existing technologies.

We deliver these services using industry-recognised IT Service Management frameworks and security controls (ITIL, COBIT, ISO27001), and cloud computing standards (ISO 17788/17789, CSA).

With decades of experience across multiple industry verticals, our security solutions architects are vendor-agnostic experts in both high and low level technical design. They combine off-the-shelf and bespoke solutions to deliver cost-effective security, tailored to meet the needs of your business' specific networks, applications, platforms, infrastructure and assets. 

Enterprise Security Architecture powerfully aligns core business strategy, security and implementation - but its heavyweight components don't always sit right with the reality of many business' budgets and resources.

This is why we have developed the totally unique ‘Architecture-in-a-Box’ approach, built on the world-renowned SABSA (Sherwood Applied Business Security Architecture) method. It’s ESA, but in streamlined, flexible, and rapidly adaptable form!

  • Cyber Security Healthcheck Services
  • Cyber Security Strategy
  • Cyber Security Consultancy Services

There are many ways your current cyber security posture can potentially open the door to an attack or breach. Our Cyber Security Health Checks use NCSC - and ISO 27001- aligned methodologies to spot them, and inform the development of an effective cyber security strategy to counter them.

A defined cyber security strategy is critical for understanding how to address both current and future threats – including the challenges presented by changing legal and regulatory landscapes. Our experts develop forward-thinking strategies (backed by many years’ experience across diverse business verticals) to create a realistic, resilient roadmap for your business’ security going forward.

Firesand’s experts can provide cyber security consulting tailored to your organisation’s precise needs - ranging from risk management programme commissioning to dedicated security architecture sub-projects.
