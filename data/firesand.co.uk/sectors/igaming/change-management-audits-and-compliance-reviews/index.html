In the United States and across Europe, many states and jurisdictions require compliance with specific change management standards or guides, such as the GLI CMP Guide. Change management is also a critical component of other often mandated standards such as GLI-19 Appendix B, GLI-33 Appendix B and ISO27001. In this highly regulated landscape, change management audits and compliance reviews are crucial for ensuring adherence to legal and regulatory requirements, avoiding potential penalties, reputational damage, and loss of customer trust.

Change management audits facilitate a structured approach to assess and manage organizational changes. Audits offer valuable insights into processes, systems, and operations, helping organizations implement effective change management strategies and maintain compliance. In the iGaming industry, Firesand Ltd has permission and holds supplier licenses issued by gaming commissions in multiple states across the United States. Our reports and audits are submitted and accepted by these gaming commissions, making us the experts in the field.
