Certification and Accreditation

Always-evolving expertise

Security is ever-changing, so we pride ourselves on staying ahead of the curve. It’s why we focus on attaining not only certifications and accreditations that are respected and recognised throughout the industry, but those that we can constantly develop, expand and fine-tune.

This ensures that we’re always ready to surmount the latest challenges, run with the latest thinking, and home in on the latest breed of threats and risks.

We are certified in everything from overall organisational security posture, to the specifics of design, software and applications, vendor- and technology-specific coding skills, audit and assessment, privacy and data protection, and enterprise-grade secure architecture.

Between us, our engineers, architects and consultants hold well over 20 industry-recognised certifications and accreditations.

Here are the certifications and accreditations that mean you can have confidence in our people and the services they deliver.

  • CISSP
  • ISSAP
  • CSSLP
  • SSP .NET
  • SSP .Java
  • CISA

Certified Information Systems Security Privacy Professional - The most esteemed cybersecurity certification in the world, for security leaders who understand cybersecurity strategy, as well as hands-on, organisation-wide implementation.

Information Systems Security Architecture Professional - Elite credentials demonstrating specialist expertise in developing, designing and analysing security solutions, and in risk-based senior management guidance, based on organisational goals.

Certified Software Security Lifecycle Professional - Global, vendor-neutral certification demonstrating leading software and application security skills, including authentication, authorisation and auditing, across each systems development life cycle phase.

Secure Software Practitioner - .NET- Constantly updated qualifications in enterprise software security and language-specific secure coding, focusing on the widely deployed .NET technology stack.

Secure Software Practitioner - JAVA - Constantly updated qualifications in enterprise software security and language-specific secure coding, focusing on Java.

Certified Information Systems Auditor - Globally recognised Information Systems audit certification. Holders are qualified to assess vulnerabilities, report on compliance and institute controls within the enterprise.

Certification Information Security Manager - Uniquely management-focused certification recognising specialists who manage, design, oversee and assess an enterprise’s information security.

Fellow of Information Privacy, International Association of Privacy Professionals
- Elite designation (there are only a handful of FIPs in the UK) demonstrating comprehensive knowledge and real-world implementation of privacy laws, privacy program management and data protection practices.

Certified Information Privacy Professional - Europe - Preeminent privacy credential, demonstrating comprehensive GDPR knowledge and understanding to ensure compliance and data protection in a European context.

Certified Information Privacy Technologist - Certifies the ability to build organisational privacy structures from the ground up, factoring data privacy into the design and delivery of every product and service.

Certified Information Privacy Manager - The world’s first and only certification in privacy program management. Holders have shown their ability to go beyond knowledge of privacy regulations and make privacy work within organisations, day to day.

SCF - SABSA Chartered Security Architect - Foundation - Comprehensive, competencies-based programme that certifies professional proficiency in all aspects of theoretical, conceptual and practical enterprise security architecture.

TOGAF® 9.1 Certified Level - The Open Group Architecture Framework - Certified credential demonstrating understanding of the core TOGAF principles of enterprise architecture, as used by the world’s leading organisations to improve business efficiency.
