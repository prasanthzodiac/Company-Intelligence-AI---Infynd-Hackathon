With over 1 million cubic square feet of storage capacity available we can offer a  solution tailored to your company’s individual document storage needs.

Switching Provider

In our time we have relocated millions of documents. If you are looking to move your records from your current storage provider to us we can make the process hassle free, cost free and with no break in service. We offer a ‘free of cost’ transfer: if the current provider charges you a  fee for the permanent withdrawal of the documents we offset this with a rent-free period.  This is how the process works:

  • We will liaise directly with the incumbent supplier.
  • Collecting a minimum of 250 boxes per day.
  • All boxes are given a barcode upon intake.
  • We provide confirmation of all items collected on a daily spreadsheet.
  • Items are securely stored, and details uploaded onto our web portal
    within 24 hours of arriving at our record centres.
  • At the end of the transfer period we will reconcile received items
    against the original list to ensure complete and successful handover.
  • Your dedicated account manager will handle any discrepancies on
    your behalf to ensure a hassle-free transfer.
