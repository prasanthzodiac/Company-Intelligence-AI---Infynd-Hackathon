      Document Storage at State Of The Art Secure Records Centres

      The Hill Company owns and runs 6 purpose-built document storage centres. All have the highest level of security & fire protection, and purpose-built modular racking.  Three are in Essex, within easy reach of London.  Together the centres have over 1 million cubic feet of storage capacity and are managing over 3 million boxes of documents for businesses across the UK.

      We offer document storage solutions tailored to your company’s individual needs, including:

      • ‘Live active’ for files that are frequently accessed
      • Durasteel vault storage for documents requiring the highest level of security and fire protection.
      • Deep storage for cost-effective storage of rarely accessed files.
      • Scan on demand to deliver documents to your desk within three hours

      Storing and Accessing your Documents

      Each client is assigned a dedicated storage bay for their documents, which never changes. Any items retrieved are always returned to the same location, reducing the risk of loss or misplacement. Unlike providers who rely on ‘random storage’ systems, our approach ensures consistency and accurate tracking.

      All document collections, retrievals, and deliveries can be managed easily through our secure online portal.

      Deliveries and collections are carried out exclusively by our own branded, GPS-tracked vehicles, operated by uniformed staff. Every item is scanned at each stage using mobile scanners, guaranteeing full traceability throughout the process.

      For urgent requirements, our Scan on Demand service allows documents to be scanned and sent to you, anywhere in the world, within just 3 hours.

      Active Document Storage

      We recommend Active Document Storage for records that need frequent access. These documents are available to clients 24/7, all year round. They can be returned the same day or the next day.  Alternatively, they can be scanned and securely delivered in digital format via our online portal, to anywhere with internet access, within hours.

      Wills and Deeds

      For critical documents such as wills and deeds we offer enhanced storage within Durasteel fire-resistant vault. Each vault is humidity controlled, fully alarmed, and continuously CCTV monitored. Access is strictly limited to authorised personnel using swipe-card entry systems.

      For extra protection, the vaults are equipped with Inergen fire suppression systems, which quickly lower oxygen levels to halt combustion, typically extinguishing a fire within 40 seconds. This residue-free gas is completely safe for people and the environment.

      Where individual deed packets are required we can arrange delivery within sealed crates.

      Clients have 24/7 access to their stored documents, 365 days a year, with same-day or next-day delivery options available, plus we offer rapid access online via our secure digital portal.

      There’s more about Will Storage on our dedicated will storage website.

      Long Term Storage

      Our long-term document storage, also known as deep storage, provides a secure and cost-effective solution for files that need to be retained for legal or compliance purposes but are rarely accessed.

      This service is ideal for archived records, historical files, and documents requiring extended retention periods.

      All items are safely stored off-site in our secure facility and can be retrieved and returned to you within two working days whenever required..

      Digital Media Storage

      We securely store all types of digital media including:

      • External hard drives: hard disks (HDDs) and solid-state drives (SSDs)
      • USB memory sticks
      • Memory cards: SD, microSD, CompactFlash etc
      • Optical discs: CD Roms, DVDs and Blu-ray discs
      • Tapes : DAT/DDS, DLT and LTO

      All items are stored within Durasteel vaults, which are:

      • Fully alarmed
      • Fully access controlled
      • CCTV monitored
      • Have Inergen fire suppression systems
      • Humidity controlled

      Lockable transit cases are available to purchase or rent.

      All your items in our secure storage are accessible 24/7/365 and can be delivered back to you on the same day or the next day.

      Fire Protection

      All Hill Company record management centres are equipped with advanced fire protection systems, which safeguard the documents stored with us. These include:

      • VESDA (Very Early Smoke Detection Apparatus): the
        system constantly samples the air within the centre. Any significant
        change in conditions immediately triggers a warning to the operations team, including a zonal reference.
      • Zonal monitoring with Redcare: If an alarm is activated, the system automatically notifies the appropriate emergency services.
      • Zonal sprinkler system: Sprinklers operate only in the affected area, preventing unnecessary risk to the wider archive.
      • Inergen gas protected vaults: Our sealed Durasteel vaults, designed for business-critical records such as deeds, wills, and digital media, are safeguarded with Inergen gas. In the event of fire, the gas is released to reduce oxygen levels and extinguish flames without damaging documents.
      • Fire Safety Equipment: Fire extinguishers are located throughout our storage facilities.
      • Trained Fire Marshals: On-site marshals are fully trained in the safe use of fire extinguishers.
      •  No Smoking Policy: Smoking is strictly prohibited across all Hill Company facilities.
