What is Data Annotation?

Data annotation is the essential process of labelling raw data – images, videos, text, or audio – to transform it into structured, machine-readable information.  Adding meaningful tags and labels ensures that AI models are trained to understand and interpret data accurately, driving precise predictions and smarter decisions. High-quality data annotation is the key to unleashing the full potential of AI.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of data annotation services does Aya Data offer?
Aya Data provides a comprehensive suite of data & AI annotation services tailored to diverse AI needs. Our expertise spans image and video labelling, including advanced techniques like instance segmentation and 3D point cloud annotation. In natural language processing, we cover everything from named entity recognition to sentiment analysis and specialised tasks like legal document annotation.
How does Aya Data ensure the quality of its data annotation?
We maintain quality through AI and expert reviews, regular annotator training, and real-time process updates based on project metrics.
What sets Aya Data's annotation services apart from competitors?
At Aya Data, we use a combination of skilled medical experts and advanced AI tools to ensure the highest level of accuracy. All annotations are reviewed by qualified professionals to meet strict industry standards (ISO).
What are data annotation services?
AI data annotation services involve labeling raw data, such as images, videos, text or audio, to make it machine-readable for training AI and machine learning models. High-quality annotations ensure accurate predictions and smarter AI systems.
What types of data annotation services does Aya Data offer?
Aya Data is able to annotate images, text, audio and video. Our comprehensive annotation services include:

A. Computer vision annotation: Bounding boxes, polygon annotation, image segmentation, keypoint annotation.

B. Natural language annotation: Named entity recognition (NER), sentiment analysis, audio/text translation, Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback (RLHF).

C. 3D annotation: 3D bounding boxes, 3D polygon annotation, 3D semantic segmentation, 3D point cloud labeling.
Why are data annotation services important for AI and machine learning?
Accurate data annotation is essential for training AI models to recognize patterns, classify data and make predictions. It helps ensure that AI-powered systems deliver precise and reliable results across all industries.
Why Aya Data?
