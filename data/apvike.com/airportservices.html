Fuel Ticket Automation:

Fuel Ticket Automation

.

Many of the Into-Plane Services providers, even today, rely on paper-based fuel tickets or Fuel Delivery Voucher (FDV). This paper based process poses many challenges in successfully refueling an aircraft in a timely manner. It becomes challenging and time consuming to let the fuel delivery engineer know about the latest changes in the flight schedule, changes to the fuel load, aircraft or gate. Furthermore, the engineer has to leave the dispenser ramp to deliver the completed ticket / voucher to the cockpit or dispatch. Thus, every activity that is paper based opens the door for delays. This process also calls for human errors and inefficacies inherent to the paper-based systems.

Apvike's Fuel Ticket Automation Solution assists in overcoming those challenges and delivers real-time information to fuelers enabling them to be at the aircraft for the actual fuel delivery thereby significantly improving the Turn Around Time (TAT) of the fuel delivery services.

Highlights of Apvike's Fuel Ticket Automation Solution:

  • Comprehensive digitalization of traditional into-plane Fuel Ticket Automation.

  • The software runs on any tablet (e.g. with or without ATEX certification) with an operating system of your choice.

  • Seamlessly integrates with any backend software that you have standardized on.

  • Savings in TAT of around 12+ minutes per traditional fueling time.

  • Eliminates human errors.

  • Significantly reduce misfueling incidents while fuel delivery.

  • Generates Invoicing data automatically.

  • Cloud based / Premise based flexible licencing options.

  • Superior flight management, safety improvements.

    Self Help Software Portal:

    Self Help Software Portal:

    .

    Self Help Software Portal is a web-based, enterprise class, secure, role based software framework. It assists in moving away from traditional paper-work and adopt digitalization with an objective of improving business agility. This is a comprehensive software framework that can be quickly tailor made as per customer's requirements. The basic framework of the software assists supervisors in assigning tasks to their teammates while the teammates can report the status of each of the tasks on a daily basis to the supervisor while they actaully execute them. The framwork can trigger alerts based on certain conditions set by the users as a part of reminder mechanism. The portal also delivers variety of reports for effectively managing operations. The framwork also has built-in Document management capabilities.
