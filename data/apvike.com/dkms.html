AI/ML enabled Document & Knowledge Management Software: Overview

These days, many organizations are riding on the wave of digitalization powered by AI/ML. Going paperless or at least minimizing the usage of paper is one the initialtives that naturally gets top priority. But along with it comes another challenge of HOW to go about it...HOW soon (if not WHEN!)... and WHAT should be the budget!

The Document & Knowledge Management system is not a new subject in the industry. The system deals with organizing official documents at a centralized location with proper security and appropriate controls for managing these documents effectively. When these documents are project specific, organizations expect to capture & manage the right level of Knowledge shared by respective stakeholders. The Knowledge, when managed well, will help in avoiding the situation of "reinventing the wheel" thereby significantly reducing overheads, cost and time.

With our industry experience, we have come up with an AI/ML powered Enterprise class Software solution viz: sparkSHARE™ that can assist in effectively managing Documents and Knowledge across functions and business units. This truely enterprise class software solution supports multi-tenancy and provides secured access to Documents and Knowledge that are managed centrally (central repository database, encrypted with AI/ML techniques).
