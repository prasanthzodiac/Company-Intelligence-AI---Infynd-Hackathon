Enablement Program : 1

Skills Improvement Program cum Workshop on EPM:

Start Date: Any
Duration: Flexible (typically of 10 to 50 Hours) - spanned over the week ends or week days.


Apvike® is introducing an enablement program exclusively for students that are looking for SIP (Skills Improvement Program) and management skills enhancement in the domain of EPM (Enterprise Performance Management). The program provides with all the tools necessary to leverage your potential to become well aware of EPM. In a challenging but nurturing environment, it builds a deep understanding of business fundamentals in all major functional areas, while emphasizing the skills and abilities needed to recognize and adapt to emerging trends in EPM and new technologies in a specific industry or function.

The objectives of this program:
  • Enhance analytical skills backed by management concepts in the space of Business Analytics.
  • Understand basic and advance concepts of Enterprise Performance Management.
  • Provide Practical / Hands-on experience in the relevant area with the help of interactive workshop.

    • Following are the brief of Workshop that the participants will undergo:
  • Understand EPM – Enterprise Performance Management – System.
  • Overview of Business Analytics from Business Management point of view.
  • Discuss real life cases in Enterprise / Business Performance Management.
  • Practical exposure on Balanced Scorecard Software Solution.

    • Successful candidates will be offered with the Participation Certificate.
    The students successfully completing this program may qualify for directly applying for suitable job opportunities with Apvike® in future.
    We welcome your interest in the program and invite you to explore what this unique program can do for you and your career.

    If you are a student studying in your final semester (final year), you can also directly enrol for this program.
