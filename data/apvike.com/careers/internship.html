Trainee - Data Analyst (Data & Algorithms):

Opportunity Code: TDA
Start Date: Flexible
Duration: 2 to 6 months (Flexible)
Expected educational background: Final year Engineering in Computer Engineering (or equivalent)
Hands on with C / C++ / Python
Knowledge of mathematical and statistical algorithms

Objectives:

1. To provide an opportunity to understand real-life cases and challenges.
2. To provide perspectives for solving these challenges.
3. To improve skills and knowledge.
4. To boost the confidance w.r.t. the domain.

Brief about the program:

You will be responsible for carrying out data analysis , statistical analysis and generating, configuring and maintaining 'Data Sets' derived out of algorithmic Intelligence. You will assist and contribute in developing various 'Data Sets' and 'Test Cases' for numerous business scenarios. Hypothesis building, Data Visualization and Data exploration would be the areas where you are expected to research, explore, learn, develop and contribute.
Hands on with Python, R and allied technologies to develop basic programs is a must.

Hands on with Databases such MySQL will be added advantage.

Knowledge of various methematical and statistical algorithms is essential.

You are expected to conduct research for generating pragmatic data sets in order to carryout numerous tests and validations.

You must have knowledge of statistical methodologies and algorithms and should apply that knowledge to help develop and test business cases.

Business Development (Trainee):

Opportunity Code: TBDE
Start Date: Flexible
Duration: 2 to 6 months (Flexible)
Expected educational background: Final year graduation or equivalent.
Hands on experience in using MS-Office applications (or equivalent).
Exceptional learning attitude with an eye on details.
Good presentation skills with fluency in the local language besides English.

Objectives:

1. To provide an opportunity to understand real-life business cases and challenges.
2. To provide perspectives for solving these business challenges.
3. To improve skills and knowledge.
4. To boost the confidence w.r.t. the domain.

Brief about the program:


Do you have an attitude of a rainmaker? Then this is THE opportunity for you!
You will learn to contribute in driving Apvike’s IT- Business Products, Solutions and professional consulting services in India; essentially for the revenue generation. You will learn to contribute in developing business strategies that integrates and co-ordinates Apvike's resources, key stakeholders and manage program opportunities with a career progression to a business management (P&L ownership) role.

You will be responsible to:

  • develop plans and integrated strategies with a detailed eye on effective lead conversion ratio.
  • drive selected accounts and projects and “go-to-market” communications comprised of industry solutions and service-offerings, to achieve opportunity win metrics.
  • convey “Why Apvike” to prospects / customers (across the table or virtually) from both business and technical perspectives.
  • lead the development of strategic presentations and proposals to position Apvike’s value and advantage to the customer.
  • develop opportunity pipeline, references / case studies, investment options and business development / opportunity pursuit best practices.
  • participate in demonstrating products and solutions to the prospects as and when required.

    ESG Researcher:

    Opportunity Code: ESGR
    Start Date: Flexible
    Duration: 2 to 6 months (Flexible)
    Expected educational background: Semi-Final Year / Final year graduation or equivalent.
    Hands on experience in using MS-Office applications, especially Excel(or equivalent).
    Exceptional learning and research attitude with an eye on details.
    Good presentation and documentation skills with fluency in the local language besides English.

    What your will experience:

    You’ll join the ESG team at Apvike, working on challenging projects for about 12 week period. Working closely with the Environment and Social Impact Manager, you’ll be exposed to a range of exciting and impactful projects.
    For example:

  • Environment & Social
    • Studying climate strategy of Fortune 1000 Fintech, Aviation and Pharma companies in line with ESG regulations
  • Social Impact
    • Through extensive research, identify business cases of Fortune 1000 Fintech, Aviation and Pharma companies
  • ESG Reports
    • Conduct extensive study of ESG reports of Fortune 1000 Fintech, Aviation and Pharma companies.

    What does it take to qualify?

    Qualification:

  • You graduate or have graduated from university in 2023/2024/2025 with a Bachelors degree (we don’t mind what you have studied)
  • You have a strong interest in ESG, sustainability and social impact and may have some evidence of this through your study, society membership or volunteering opportunities
  • You are always curious and enjoy researching and applying critical thinking to your findings
  • You are data driven and have excellent analytical skills, ideally with a good knowledge of Excel and other MS-Office applications
  • You have exceptional verbal & written English skills - communication is a big part of this role and you’ll need to feel confident in presenting your work
  • You are meticulous with detail and organised by nature, with the ability to manage multiple projects
