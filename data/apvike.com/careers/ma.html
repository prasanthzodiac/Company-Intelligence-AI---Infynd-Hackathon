Market Analyst

Market Analyst (Market Research and Lead Generation)

Opportunity Code: MA-24
Start Date: ASAP
Expected relevant industry experience: 6 months to 1.0 years
Expected mobility: None / Hybrid mode option available
Package: Negotiable...industry at par
NOTE: Desire to analyse and research information for prospect identification and hunting is a MUST.


Do you have an eye for details and love to find potential buyers? Then this is THE opportunity for you!
This position will drive Apvike’s Enterprise Software Products and Solutions in the space of Enterprise Performance Management, Industrial Process Automation / Industrial IoT and Into-Plane Services automation in domestic as well as European region. Key attributes needed for this role include secondary market research, data analysis and synthesis with exceptional documentation skills.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Contribute in executing market analysis (EU and South Asian regions) in terms of secondary market research, information gathering, high level research report writing and analysis of the socio-eco market dynamics.
  • Contribute in market segmentation, lead generation and prospects profiling by exploiting all possible media (telephone/ email/ chat/ web/ digital and social media etc.)
  • Contribute in business development projects.
  • Readiness to work under strict timelines and business pressure is a must.
  • Generate qualified leads as per assigned targets-month on month basis.

    • Desired Skills & Experience:

  • Graduation in Economics and Political Sciences
  • Articulate with an eye for details
  • Must have ability to chase monthly targets towards lead generation
  • Good understanding of enterprise software solutions
  • Excellent understanding of economics and industry
  • Ability to communicate clearly, concisely and effectively in all correspondence (verbal, written, presentations, etc.)
  • Creative, energetic, pleasant personality with go-getter attitude with an eye over quality in execution
  • Must have hands-on experience with MS-Office application (word, excel, power point) or equivalent
  • Must be able to create, publish and manage blogs / articles / short write-ups with reasonable technical depth in accordance with market trends
  • Knowledge of German language (at least for reading) will be added advantage though not a mandate
  • Must have good communication skills
  • Must have excellent writing / documentation skills

    • If you wish to apply for this opportunity, email your detailed CV (with covering letter) to: career@apvike.com
    Make sure to write the relevant Opportunity Code (MA-24) in the subject line of your email.
