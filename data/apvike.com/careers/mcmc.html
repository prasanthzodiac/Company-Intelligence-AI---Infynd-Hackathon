Marketing Content Management Consultant
Marketing Content Management Consultant:

Opportunity Code: MCMC-24
Start Date: ASAP
Expected relevant industry experience: 6 months to Max 1.0 Year
Expected mobility: None / Hybrid mode option available
Package: Negotiable...industry at par

If you are highly passionate in creating quality contents that engage target audience; then this is THE opportunity for you!
As one of the team’s storytellers, you’ll deliver compelling content that showcases our passion and expertise in areas of Information Technology & Management, Industry 4.0, IT/Business Consulting etc.
While you create articles and/or blogs that showcase our capabilities that explore the difference our work makes to wider corporate audience; no two days will be the same!
In short, we’re looking for someone who is a self-starter, confident in ensuring the regular creation and delivery of high-quality written marketing content.

What will you be doing?:

  • You’ll be on the lookout for the hot topics across our priority markets and service offerings, ensuring the content created is timely and optimised to deliver impact
  • Writing and delivering engaging and informative content.
  • Interviewing internal team of experts to harvest insights and expertise that can be turned into engaging marketing contents
  • Posting the content you create on our website and social media platforms
  • Working with our in-house creative team to provide copywriting support to compliment the visual impact of their materials
  • Providing reasonable ad-hoc marketing support

    • Required Skills::

  • Relevant degree or professional marketing qualifications would be helpful, but not essential
  • A journalistic mindset to go out into the practice and seek out stories and deliver thought leadership style articles and content
  • Possess a fluent, confident, engaging writing style and consistent tone of voice
  • A joined-up approach, with a view to how content created can be repurposed and extract maximum value
  • An ability to drive the delivery of marketing campaigns
  • Confident in writing for web and social media and print
  • A keen eye for detail
  • Strong acumen of content writing in areas of Information Technology & Management, Industry 4.0, IT consultancy etc.
