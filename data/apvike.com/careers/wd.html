Web Development & Media Consultant
Web development and Media Consultant:

Opportunity Code: WD-24
Start Date: ASAP
Expected relevant industry experience: 1.0 Year to 2.0 Years
Expected mobility: None / Hybrid mode option available
Package: Negotiable...industry at par

If you are highly creative and passionate in managing digital media including websites for end customer delight and executing marketing strategies then this is THE opportunity for you!
This position will contribute in executing Apvike’s Digital Marketing strategy targeting India, EU and UK regions; essentially for creating exceptional customer experience.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Plan, Design and Develope digital media and web pages that effectively integrates marketing strategies
  • Eye on effective lead conversion ratio.
  • Create high performing Web embedded features for exceptional mobile and desktop experience
  • Ensure exceptional user experience on mobile and desktop.
  • End-to-end Ownership of the media / web development

  • Interested in working in a high-performing work environment with a high learning curve
  • Previous start-up experience or contribution to any open source project is preferred
  • Expert in designing responsive web forms, HTML/CSS, JavaScripting
  • Very good at PHP and developing MySQL database connections to web applications
  • Basic Knowledge of Bootstrap, JavaScript, Jquery, JSON, Ajax,Angular framework etc.
  • Basic knowledge of GDPR compliance and web cookies management etc.
  • Basic Knowledge of media (video) creation and editing (though not a mandate)
  • Ability to work with RESTful APIs ( though not a mandate)
  • Demonstrate a strong sense of urgency in solving problems, digging in deep to identify specific needs and solutions
  • Exceptional learning attitide
