About Apvike

We collaborate with our customers to deliver innovative yet sustainable software solutions.

Apvike (literally pronounced as "App-V-K") is a team of experts in making innovative enterprise software solutions in the space of Enterprise Performance Management, Information / Data Management, Industrial Process Automation and Into-Plane and allied Services Automation. While we do so, sustainability is always in our mind. With innovative ideas, industry proven methodologies and our tacit knowledge, we develop future-proof, forward looking yet pragmatic business software coupled with highly competent professional services. With these high quality software solutions and our professional services, we partner with our customers to enable them in making informed decisions and optimise their process performance.
We are focusing the region of UK, EU and Indian subcontinent for providing right solutions to the business challenges of our customers.

Values

Our Operating Principle: To Deliver innovative and sustainable solutions.

At Apvike, our day-to-day work attributes to these principles:

  • Solutions- We strive to find right answers for our customers's business problems.

  • Economy- Our work adds significant economic benefits to our customers.

  • Appreciation- We appreciate the uniqueness of our customers, partner and associates.

  • Timeliness- We value our customers' time and go extra mile for timely delivery.

  • Quality- We deliver sustainable solutions and services with exceptional high quality.   • Knowledge- We believe that sharing and gaining knowledge is a continuous process. We apply our experience to nurture our solutions and services.
