Most Alvao websites use cookies. Cookies are small text files placed on your device to store data so web servers can use it later. Alvao, eventually third-party use cookies to remember your preferences and settings, help you sign in, show you personalized ads, and analyze how well our websites are working. For more info, see the Cookies and similar technologies section of the Privacy Statement.

We use functional cookies to perform essential website functions. For example, they're used to log you in, save your language preferences, improve performance, route traffic between web servers, detect the size of your screen, determine page load times, improve user experience, and for audience measurement. These cookies are necessary for our websites to work.

We allow third parties to use analytics cookies to understand how you use our websites so we can make them better and the third parties can develop and improve their products, which they may use on websites that are not owned or operated by Alvao. For example, they're used to gather information about the pages you visit and how many clicks you need to accomplish a task. We use some analytics cookies for advertising.

We and third parties use social media cookies to show you ads and content based on your social media profiles and activity on our websites. We and third parties use advertising cookies to show you new ads by recording which ads you've already seen. IT Service Management begins with connecting the right tools

We develop our products as an integrated solution because they work best together – as ITSM.

In Asset Management, you can clearly record tangible and intangible assets

Asset Management will reduce the administrative burden and make the handover of assets more transparent. Employees will be able to confirm the handover of the new equipment from anywhere – even via their phone. You can always check which assets are in their care and the parameters of the equipment entrusted.

With clear records, you know where assets are located and what is happening to them – you reduce asset management costs and can plan IT investments based on hard data.

During implementation, we will migrate your data from a manual system or Excel spreadsheets to a new register in Asset Management. We will clean all your data, getting rid of anything superfluous, so you’ll be working with only the relevant information.

Service Desk speeds up the resolution of user requests and prevents incidents

Every hour counts. Especially when a computer is down and staff cannot work, or even worse, when the banking system fails and a disaster is looming.

Service Desk will perform the critical steps needed to cope with an outage and speed up the resolution of user requests themselves – even users with zero experience will not get lost in our Service Desk. We’ve developed the ALVAO solution with an eye on user-friendliness – anyone can handle it, via smartphone or computer.

Users can see where their request is at – making your IT function more transparent, and internally clear about what needs doing; you can prioritize, as well as having more time for strategic projects.

The ITSM connection: Service Desk + Asset Management

Dealing with lots of people, lots of technology and a mailbox cluttered with user requests? With the ITSM solution, one unified point of contact natively linked to the current asset register will be created. All the necessary information for the right decision – always at hand, immediately.

ALVAO is Microsoft’s preferred solution

We develop tools exclusively for Microsoft 365, both in terms of integration and user adoption. Using our products is just like MS Office – users find it familiar straight away.

ALVAO works directly through extensions to Microsoft products already used across your business: MS Outlook and MS Teams.

ALVAO tools will strengthen your cyber security

The financial sector works with sensitive data and systems that require the highest security. ALVAO will help you secure your organization’s day-to-day running.

The combination of Service Desk & Asset Management significantly enhances cybersecurity. ALVAO is certified to ISO 27001 and SOC 2 Type II.  – an internationally valid standards vouching for the security of our products, while the products and cloud services are also OWASP methodology tested for vulnerabilities.

Power BI reports help you impress your management and vindicate your budget

Team management reports and IT performance assessments help you justify your budget expenditure as well as motivating your team – staff can see how they’re meeting goals. With Power BI, you know how well you’re meeting metrics and KPIs.

Recruitment is swift and strategic

Hiring staff, whether secretaries, developers, managers or call centre operators can be fast and efficient. ALVAO automates the onboarding/departure of employees. Upon arrival, requests are raised automatically with the relevant departments – access card provision, induction training etc. With ALVAO you won’t overlook anything important.
