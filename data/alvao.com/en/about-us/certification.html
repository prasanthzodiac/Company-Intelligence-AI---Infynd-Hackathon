Most Alvao websites use cookies. Cookies are small text files placed on your device to store data so web servers can use it later. Alvao, eventually third-party use cookies to remember your preferences and settings, help you sign in, show you personalized ads, and analyze how well our websites are working. For more info, see the Cookies and similar technologies section of the Privacy Statement.

We use functional cookies to perform essential website functions. For example, they're used to log you in, save your language preferences, improve performance, route traffic between web servers, detect the size of your screen, determine page load times, improve user experience, and for audience measurement. These cookies are necessary for our websites to work.

We allow third parties to use analytics cookies to understand how you use our websites so we can make them better and the third parties can develop and improve their products, which they may use on websites that are not owned or operated by Alvao. For example, they're used to gather information about the pages you visit and how many clicks you need to accomplish a task. We use some analytics cookies for advertising.

We and third parties use social media cookies to show you ads and content based on your social media profiles and activity on our websites. We and third parties use advertising cookies to show you new ads by recording which ads you've already seen. They're also used to show you ads that are more relevant to youbased on your social media interests and website browsing history.

Certifications & awards

We strive to protect and prevent you from cybersecurity threats. ALVAO holds several cybersecurity certifications, including ISO 27001 and SOC 2, Type 2. These rigorous international standards ensure the protection of your information and help deliver software that empowers you to meet your business objectives.

ALVAO holds the ISO 27001 security certificate

ISO/IEC 27001 is an internationally applicable standard defining Information Security Management system requirements, especially information trust management for employees, processes, IT systems and company strategy. These standards are set by the International Organisation for Standardization, known by its acronym ISO.

SOC 2, Type 2

Safety has always remained a top priority for ALVAO. The attainment of SOC 2, along with our previously acquired ISO 27001 certification, serves as further affirmation of our well-established and meticulously controlled processes in this domain.

More information

G-Cloud Supplier in the UK, Czechia and Slovakia

The British government approved and officially classified ALVAO Asset Management and Service Desk as secure solutions after meeting all of their requirements.

What is G-Cloud?

In 2013, the UK government introduced a ‘Cloud First’ policy for all technology decisions in public sector. As part of the Cloud First policy, the government launched the Digital Marketplace which is an online catalogue for public sector organisations to find technology solutions.

PinkVERIFY certified status on 7 processes

ALVAO has successfully passed the PinkVerify ITSM certification (published by Pink Elephant). Our tools meet 100% of the PinkVerify general, core, and integration suitability requirements:

  • IT Asset Management
  • Change Enablement
  • Incident Management
  • Problem Management
  • Service Request Management
  • Service Configuration Management
  • Service Catalog Management

Cyber Essentials

ALVAO is now certified under the Cyber Essentials scheme. This UK government–backed program defines a baseline level of cybersecurity for organizations, aiming to protect them from the most common online threats and reduce the risk of attacks.

 

Why is it important that our ITSM tool has those certifications?

ALVAO's certifications serve as independent verification of a solution's security and reliability. By earning these certifications, we demonstrate our commitment to robust cybersecurity, ensuring your data remains secure and compliant.
