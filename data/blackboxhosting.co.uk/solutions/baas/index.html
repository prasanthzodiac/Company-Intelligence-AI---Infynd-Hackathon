The Key Characteristics of BaaS

Centralised Data Backup

Centralising data backup with Veeam makes it easier to manage backups of virtual machines, databases, applications, operating systems and file systems in a more streamlined manner. You can retain data for longer periods of time, especially for organisations in finance and healthcare, which need longer data retention for regulatory needs.

Exceptional Security and Encryption

Data backup providers ensure your data is protected in transit as well as at rest with high security and access controls. Security measures include firewalls and intrusion protection systems, while access control measures limit who is authorised to access the data. With a robust BaaS solution, you can reduce your organisation’s risk of data breaches while complying with customisable data protection regulations and enjoy complete peace of mind around your data security.

Easy to Scale

As your business grows, so do your needs, and our managed BaaS solutions can scale alongside you. Your backups are housed within a third-party data centre, so you can increase or decrease system capacity without making changes to the existing infrastructure. Compared to on-premises backups, cloud-based backup resources can be scaled up or down quickly.

Cost-Effective BaaS

Backup as a service providers are a cost-effective solution, allowing you to reduce many of the upfront costs associated with setting up and managing a your own backup system. This minimises the need for your business to invest in hardware, software or any kind of infrastructure, as well as outsourcing the worry about costs associated with managing a data centre, network and staff. 

BlackBox Hosting’s BaaS is powered by Veeam for secure data backup and restoration, which ensures your data is safe, secure and can be easily restored. Our backups follow the 3-2-1 rule, which means keeping 3 separate backup copies of your data, with copies stored in two different forms of media, with one of these copies stored offsite (in the cloud). Our automatic recovery verification allows for “0 errors”, meaning should the worst happen, your data is secure.

How Does BaaS Work?

Data Selection

You share the data that needs to be backed up, whether it’s simple files and folders, or complex databases, virtual machines and applications.

Data Transfer and Storage

The selected data is securely transferred into our cloud and stored in a secure, redundant environment to protect from data loss and enable easy access and retrieval. 

Backup Schedule

We help you create a customised data backup schedule according to your specific recovery point objectives (RPOs). Alternatively, we can manage the scheduling process for you to ensure it aligns with your business’s needs.

Data Recovery

In the unfortunate event of data disruption, your business can initiate a recovery process through our console and restore your data to its original location or a new location. Alternatively, we can recover the data for you.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Backup as a Service necessary?

Whilst not necessary, BaaS is a good idea if you want to keep your data backed up at all times, without the upfront cost of setting up IT infrastructure and support. 

How does Backup as a Service work in cloud computing?

It involves backing up your data to a remote cloud managed by a third-party provider.

What is a backup server?

A backup server is a type of server that backs up data, applications, files, and databases on a specialised remote or in-house server. 

What is off-site backup?

Off-site backup, data and applications are stored outside the organisation or its core IT infrastructure. It secures data against malicious attacks.

What is on-site or on-premises backup?

On-site or on-premises backup is a backup system that backs up data to a local server, drive, or disc on the business’s premises.

What is managed backup?

Managed backup services create and store data backups with third-party service providers. BlackBox Hosting offers managed BaaS.

What factors influence BaaS pricing?

The pricing depends on data volume, number of devices, frequency of backups, and additional services like data retrieval and support. BlackBox Hosting offers fixed pricing on backup as a service solutions, unlike other providers, which charge based on usage.

Why should you choose a BaaS provider?

Opting for a data backup service over self-managing your disaster recovery plan ensures automated restoration of data, apps, and infrastructure in no time.
