What is Carbon Neutral Hosting and Why Do You Need It?

Carbon neutral hosting means that your physical servers are run on renewable energy, supported by energy-efficient systems, and offset by verified sustainability programmes. It does everything your traditional hosting does, but with significantly less environmental impact. 

Our eco hosting doesn’t compromise on server speed or data security, and with 99.999% uptime, we ensure your systems are both sustainably powered and managed.

Carbon Neutral Web Hosting FAQs

What makes eco hosting different from regular hosting?

When compared with regular hosting, eco hosting uses renewable energy, energy-efficient hardware, and carbon offsetting without affecting performance.

Is performance affected by using green hosting

No. All our eco platforms use enterprise-grade infrastructure designed for high uptime, speed, and reliability.

Can I migrate my existing setup to eco hosting?

Yes. Our team will assess your needs and handle the migration for a seamless and low-impact transition.

Is eco hosting more expensive?

No. Our eco hosting plans are competitively priced, with transparent costs and no usage-based surprises.

What energy sources do you use?

We rely on certified UK renewables, including solar, wind, and hydro. They can be verified through National Grid certifications.

Do you offer sustainability documentation?

Yes. You can request carbon offset reports, power sourcing certificates, and emissions data for stakeholder or audit use.

Can eco hosting help with ESG reporting?

Yes. Our infrastructure supports your ESG goals by offering verifiable environmental metrics that can be included in the annual reports or compliance statements.
