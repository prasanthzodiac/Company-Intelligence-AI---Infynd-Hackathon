What is Managed Hosting & Why Do You Need it?

 

Unlike traditional hosting, with managed hosting services, the managed hosting provider directs the setup, management, administration, security, and overall support for the server.

Signing up for a managed server is best if you are a business or a website owner who doesn’t have the resources or skillset to manage your own server setup. This allows you to focus on your core business operations while your managed server hosting provider takes care of website operations.

Why Do You Need a Managed Server?

You should consider switching to managed server hosting if:

  • You lack the resources or skillsets to manage your applications, servers or website
  • You want to reduce internal workload
  • You need maximum uptime, scalability, and security
  • Your business depends on high-priority applications or customer-facing systems
  • You need experts to handle your compliance and security standards

Managed Hosting FAQs

What’s the difference between managed hosting and unmanaged hosting?

Managed hosting services take care of server setup, monitoring, updates, security, and support on your behalf. In contrast, you have to handle all of this yourself in unmanaged hosting.

Can I migrate my existing infrastructure to your platform?

Yes, we offer complimentary migration support with minimal downtime. Our onboarding team will work with you to ensure a smooth transition.

Is managed hosting suitable for small businesses?

Yes, our managed hosting services are a great fit for all startups, SMEs, and enterprises; we cater to all business sizes as well as budgets.

What level of support do you include in managed hosting?

Get 24/7 monitoring, emergency response, and access to UK-based support engineers via phone, email, or ticketing through partnering with BlackBox Hosting’s managed servers.

Is my data secure with managed hosting?

Yes. All environments include enterprise-grade firewalls, encryption, regular patching, and intrusion detection systems.

Can I scale my environment later?

Yes, you have the complete flexibility to scale storage, RAM, bandwidth, or entire nodes on demand.
