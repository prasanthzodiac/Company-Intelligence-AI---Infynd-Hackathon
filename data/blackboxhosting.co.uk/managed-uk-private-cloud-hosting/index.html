Who We Are

At BlackBox Hosting, we deliver fully managed private cloud solutions built specifically for software providers and business-critical applications. Our UK-based infrastructure gives you the performance, resilience, and security your clients depend on, all with guaranteed uptime and expert management.

We take care of the complex parts of running and maintaining critical systems, so you don’t have to. From hosting and monitoring to proactive support and disaster recovery, our team ensures your platforms run seamlessly and securely.

Whether you’re delivering SaaS, enterprise software, or bespoke business platforms, our private cloud is designed to help you scale with confidence enabling your teams to focus on innovation and growth.
