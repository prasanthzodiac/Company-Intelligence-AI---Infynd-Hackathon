Proud to support...

Village Manchester Football Club

We proudly sponsor Village Manchester Football Club (VMFC), a team that is LGBTQ+ friendly and upholds a simple ethos: ‘Everyone is welcome, discrimination is not.

VMFC is a gay/bi/straight/trans inclusive club that welcomes anyone who loves football.

With six teams, the club is always on the lookout for new players of all abilities. Training takes place during the week, with competitive fixtures on Saturdays and Sundays. The team also competes in national tournaments and overseas trips most years.

VMFC formed in February 1996 with the aim of providing gay and bisexual guys the opportunity to play and support football in a safe and welcoming environment.

To get involved, visit their website at www.vmfc.co.uk/new

Speed of Sight

We’re also proud to parter with the amazing charity, Speed of Sight.

The Speed of Sight team runs events that empower disabled people of all ages, giving them a chance to enjoy the thrill of driving – sometimes very fast!

The have four cars, two racing cars and two all-terrain buggies, that are designed and built to give passengers a once in a life-time experience.

The charity also promotes awareness around disability too. With a range of unique experiences, and using blindfolds, they highlight the difficulty of life without one of the primary senses.

To learn more about Speed of Sight, visit their website www.speedofsight.org
