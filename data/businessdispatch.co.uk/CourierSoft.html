About CourierSoft

CourierSoft provides a total solution to courier companies who provide same-day, overnight, international courier services and also who work with third party carriers. CourierSoft offers CS Enterprise for standard courier operations, CS Franchise for franchisor/franchisee setup, CS NonTouch for Parcel and Carrier Management operations, CS eCom for courier e-commerce business.

Couriersoft provide web based application which include job booking, quotation, bar code label printing, PODs, tracking and management reports. CourierSoft comes with no setup costs and no regular on-going software maintenance or upgrade costs. Customers can use CourierSoft on pay per job basis or a monthly payment basis. The service embrace the latest satellite navigation and mobile technology to provide greater visibility of parcels and drivers in real-time.

CourierSoft provides a complete solution for office and field based operations. CourierSoft Office can be accessed on a Desktop PC with a web browser with internet connection, while the CourierSoft mobile can be deployed on PDA with a wireless Internet connection. CourierSoft application is based on the concept of ‘cloud computing’ a cost effective solution delivering explicit service at a fraction of the cost compared to an in-house system. The cloud computing technology coupled with secure software application provides around the clock access to your business from any part of the globe with an Internet connection. CourierSoft ensures the business data is backed up to eliminate any ambiguity where Data security is crucial to any successful business operation.

CS Office

CS Office provides your business with a total solution from job logging through to completion of delivery and invoicing. Map based visibility of parcels, vehicles and courier in real-time. CourierSoft also provides customer web portal for your customers to book a job and track the job status and history. CourierSoft Office enables you to log job details against predefined customer information. Once a job is logged, CourierSoft allocates the job to the best-fit driver with vehicle. Job details are then communicated to the driver via PDA (smart phone) or phone. At the point of delivery a signature is captured, POD is generated and emailed to the customer. Finally the completed jobs are invoiced by the system and emailed to the customer. Also the invoice details can be uploaded to account applications by CourierSoft.

CS Mobile

CS Mobile is a pioneering PDA based solution to help you manage your field based operations. It embraces satellite navigation and mobile technology to give you visibility of field operations in real time through CS Office. The PDA uses a wireless internet connection but can work offline Job details are communicated from CS Office via cloud technology to CS Mobile (PDA). Once logged on, deliveries and collections information can be viewed in the live jobs folder and signatures on collection and delivery can be captured. Additional instructions and phone numbers can be added to the job details. The PDA communicates at predefined intervals and updates CS Office at the end of every activity.

  • Features
    • The application is form based, which can be operated in an online and offline mode
    • Live Jobs and Archived Jobs can be viewed
    • Job priorities can be viewed and rearranged
    • Secured user access
    • Remote management of the PDA
    • Navigation facility available
    • Real-time updates and response to changing events
  • Benefits
    • Customer signature captured on delivery
    • Faster response times towards deliveries and collections
    • Drivers are well informed on job details
    • Improved accuracy of field data in real time
    • Real-time updating of data shared across enterprise.
    • Reduces administrative costs associated with time-consuming paperwork and phone calls

CS Franchise

CS Franchise is for established courier companies to expand their operations and brand name to other geographical areas using the CS Franchise application where the Franchisor can manage and monitor all the Franchisees operations, where the Franchisee can only manage and monitor their dedicated business operations.

CS NonTouch

CS NonTouch concept is for courier companies running business for number of years or with large client base like to come out from regular collection and delivery activities, and would like to offer an alternate courier service using third party carrier companies to carry out the collection and delivery. The customer will be priced and charged by the courier company. This operation is fully automated from customer booking a job via online or uploading a file containing hundreds of jobs into CourierSoft. Logging a job, generating bar-code label, assigning the job and up to POD is totally automated, through an integrated application (API) between CourierSoft and third party carrier.

CS eCom

Companies carrying out courier business can use CS eCom to cater for customers who want to get a quick quotation online in real-time, also those customers who want to book a job and pay online without taking your resource time CS eCom is integrated with online secured payment facility i.e WorldPay, Paypal so that the customer can make a secure payment to your account. Also it will save time in chasing for payment and help your cashflow. CS eCom is a supporting product for CS Enterprise
