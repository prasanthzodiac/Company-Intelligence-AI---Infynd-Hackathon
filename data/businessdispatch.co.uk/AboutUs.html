About US

Leading Software Provider Since - 2002.

Founded in 2002, BDL developed its first logistics software application for the logistics industry, named Logistics Software (LS).

Building on the success of LS, BDL expanded into large-scale application development for field service operations, creating Service Management Software (SMS).

Leveraging the experience gained from LS and SMS, BDL's software team tackled the courier industry with the introduction of CourierSoft (CS).
Recognising the need for a comprehensive solution in patient transport operations, our team developed Patient Transport Software (PTS).
Identifying a further need for tracking vehicle activities to complement these products, BDL developed a vehicle tracking application. This application can integrate with any of the aforementioned products or function independently. The complete package includes vehicle tracking software, GPS tracking hardware, and a mobile network for data transfer.

Our software architecture adheres to the principle of 'Minimum Effort – Maximum Result' for the end user. These solutions effectively monitor and manage the activities of both human resources and vehicles, delivering significant benefits and cost savings to our clients.
BDL's business strategy is centred on building long-term relationships. Committed to quality, creativity, and sound business ethics, we consider our work complete only when the client is fully satisfied.

Since 2004, we have been accredited with ISO 9001:2008 certification by TUV UK QA.
