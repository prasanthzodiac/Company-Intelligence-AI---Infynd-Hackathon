About us

By combining the knowledge of industry experts with the brightest minds in software development, we've created the best training management platform available – Bud. We've also created a company that’s full of ambitious, enthusiastic individuals who are thriving in their teams, from administration and finance, business development and marketing, product & software development and testing, and of customer success and support. Together we are Bud, and we are all very proud of everything that we’ve achieved.

Heather Frankham

Co-Founder

It’s fair to say that I’ve witnessed first-hand the growing pains that apprenticeship delivery has experienced over the past quarter century. From my beginnings as a teacher, I went on to build the UK’s largest apprenticeship provider, Lifetime, starting from scratch in 1995 before selling the business in 2016.

I’m passionate about giving young people the best possible start, and wholeheartedly believe that apprenticeships are a force for good. However, it is an industry beset with issues and that is why I decided to create Bud – to simplify training delivery and support the sector, giving managers total visibility of their organisation to reduce business risk and ultimately improve the quality of training.

Bud has achieved a huge amount in a very short period of time. Our customers and thousands of learners are using the platform every day and have given overwhelmingly positive feedback. I am confident that Bud will provide the technical support that training providers need to enable to them to continue improving outcomes for young people and adults.

David Foster

Co-Founder

During my career I have worked in a senior leadership position across a number of exciting businesses including working with Heather at Lifetime from 2009 to 2012.

Bud came about through identifying that the sector was crying out for a high-quality technology solution to automate training delivery. As a Founder, I truly believe that Bud will drive much needed improvements across the training sector, reducing administrative burdens, raising standards and enabling training providers to recognise cost savings so that investment can be made to improve the quality of training. Ultimately improving outcomes for young people and adults.

Meeting the standards

As experts in state-of-the-art technologies we strive to ‘embed a security’ first culture throughout our organisation. We are extremely proud to have achieved ISO 27001, the international standard on how to manage information security, which is evidence that the information assets we hold are secure. In order to maintain our certification, we review, maintain and continually improve our ISMS.

At Bud we hold Cyber Essentials Plus certification – this is only awarded to organisations whose security systems have stood up to vulnerability tests carried out by cyber security experts.
