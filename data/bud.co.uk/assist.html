What is Bud Assist?

As part of our Enterprise level subscription, Bud Assist is our first AI-powered feature. It’s divided into two distinct components, each serving a unique purpose. Together, they’re designed to help training providers become more efficient and proactive in their daily operations.

Chatbot

Overview
Chatbot integrates with the platform to provide tutors with a responsive and knowledgeable assistant, offering quick and accurate responses to queries. Securely hosted in the UK, it ensures data privacy while using AI trained on key industry artifacts to enhance teaching efficiency and support.

    Key Benefits

  • The Chatbot is securely hosted in the UK, ensuring data privacy and security. Customers can trust that their data is handled with the utmost care and confidentiality.

  • The AI technology powering the Chatbot has been trained on key industry artefacts, making it highly relevant and useful for training providers.
  • The Chatbot enables smooth interaction with tutors by delivering rapid and precise responses to their questions. This improves the teaching experience and boosts efficiency by providing immediate support and feedback recommendations.

Insights

Overview
The Bud algorithm, based on machine learning, analyses learner
data to provide valuable insights and recommendations. These
insights help training providers take proactive measures to
improve learner outcomes.

    Key Benefits

  • AI Insights use critical statistical indicators to segment learners and predict those at risk of early withdrawal. This allows for timely interventions, potentially reducing dropout rates.

  • The insights include recommended actions that training providers can take to address issues promptly. This helps in improving learner engagement and success rates.
  • Providers can identify learners requiring immediate attention in seconds, ensuring that no one falls through the cracks.

Bud Assist VS Bud Mark

Not to be confused with Bud Assist, Bud Mark is our game-changing, AI-powered tool, designed to revolutionise the marking and feedback process for training providers. With Bud Mark, you’ll experience a more efficient, consistent, and transparent way to assess learner performance. Its intuitive features streamline the entire marking cycle, allowing trainers to focus on delivering quality feedback rather than getting bogged down by administrative tasks.
