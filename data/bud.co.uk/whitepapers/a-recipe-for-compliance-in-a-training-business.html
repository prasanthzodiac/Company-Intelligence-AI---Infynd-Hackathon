A recipe for compliance in a training business

One of the least appealing prospects for an apprenticeship provider is the dreaded audit.

Often coming when you least expect it, the news that someone will be showing up in a few short days can leave you frantically shuffling papers and tidying up spreadsheets in a bid to provide proof you are acting by the book.

An apprenticeship programme isn’t simply about delivering learning, it’s about proving that the course you’re providing is valuable to employers and learners alike, plus keeping evidence to show that you qualify for all of government funding you’re claiming.

Download our whitepaper for specialist insight from compliance expert David Lockhart-Hawkins on setting up your apprenticeship to be compliant from its very core, and ensuring the right checks are in place.

Then you’ll have far less to worry about when it comes to audit time.
