What is Bud Mark?

Why Bud Mark?

Reduce Marking Time 
AI handles the repetitive tasks, so trainers can focus on delivering training.

 

Enhance Feedback Quality
Rubrics (marking framework) and AI suggestions ensure consistent and objective responses.

 

Boost Learner Engagement
Faster, personalised feedback keeps learners motivated and engaged.
