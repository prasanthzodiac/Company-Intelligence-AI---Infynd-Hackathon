Introducing Bud’s Enterprise tier

24 September 2024
   │  
10 AM or 2 PM
   │  
Zoom

Join us for an insightful webinar introducing Bud's new Enterprise tier and the latest features, designed to elevate your experience further and maximise efficiency. During the webinar, Bud’s product experts will walk you through the recent updates, showcase live demonstrations of the new tools, and answer all your burning questions.

What’s more, the webinar will be split into four specialised rooms, each focusing on a theme that reflects the new functionality. 

Insights Room

Dive into Bud’s newest QAR (Qualification Achievement Rates) and Benchmarking reports. Explore how the features can streamline your data analysis processes, providing insights and performance comparisons. Join Shannon Dearnley and Craig Robinson for an insightful demonstration of these features and ensure accuracy and compliance.

AI Room

After the successful completion of our AI pilot, the Bud Assist: Chatbot and Learner Insights are here to stay! Discover how these AI-powered tools can increase the efficiency and effectiveness of your training provision. Join Nick Hodder and Jamie Lewis for an exclusive demonstration and learn how Bud Assist can improve your success and dropout rates.

Learning Content Room

Explore the expanded capabilities within Bud’s Activity Library in Learning Plan and Content Insights. Chris Greatorex and Katy Hopwood will reveal how you can enhance the training experience for both trainers and learners, creating dynamic and interactive learning environments.

Enrolment Room

Get a first look at Bud’s FEQ (Flexible Enrolment Questions) and Customisable Self-Enrolment page features. In this room, Matt Wood and Alistair Wakefield will cover how you can simplify your enrolment processes to keep your learners informed, and necessary information stored securely.

Can't decide on a room? 

With both morning and afternoon sessions available, you'll have the flexibility to select multiple rooms upon registration or switch rooms during the webinar.

Whether you’re focused on Insights, AI, Enrolment, or Learning, our personalised sessions will allow you to dive deep into the areas that matter most to you.
