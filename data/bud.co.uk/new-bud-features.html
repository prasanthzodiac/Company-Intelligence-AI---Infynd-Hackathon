New Bud features 

Discover the latest functionality that's available to try on the Bud platform. Designed specifically to elevate your training experience and maximise productivity, these new tools will help you gain actionable insights, drive efficiency and enhance your internal processes.

Available now

Activity Library in Learning Plan - Enhance the training experience for both trainers and learners, streamlining the process of adding activities from an existing activity library directly into a Learning Plan, ensuring consistency and efficiency.

QAR Suite - Leverage the official specifications and rules provided by the Education and Skills Funding Agency (ESFA) by monitoring, reconciling, and analysing your qualification achievement, ensuring accuracy and compliance.

Content Insights - Gather valuable feedback from learners about the activities within their learning plans. Gain insights with the rate and comment features and enhance the educational experience of all your learners.

AI Suite - Leverage the power of AI with Bud Assist Chatbot and Bud Assist Learner Insights. Increase the efficiency and effectiveness of your training provision, improving your learner engagement, success rates, and dropout rates.

Customisable Self-Enrolment Page - Personalise the enrolment process for your applicants with bespoke content for each programme. Ensure applicants receive relevant and specific information from the start, enhancing their initial experience.

Coming soon...

Flexible Enrolment Questions - Enhance your enrolment processes with this new tool. Customise programme questions to meet specific programme requirements, ensuring that all necessary information is captured efficiently and stored securely.

As we roll out the new features, you will have the opportunity to try them for free until September. Simply fill out the form below, and a member of our Customer Success team will be in touch to switch on the available features for you and your team. 

*No card or additional information required. Trial will be enabled by your Customer Success Manager. Trial will automatically end on the 30th of September.
