The Stakes

Proving the success of your application owner program is critical

Managing a portfolio of enterprise applications is challenging, prompting many organizations to adopt application owner programs. However, these programs often struggle due to disengaged owners and limited visibility. Without effective tracking, it's hard to ensure owners meet their responsibilities, leading to a lack of confidence and a false sense of security.

Incomplete tracking creates security gaps

Difficulty in tracking which applications are managed and their current status leads to gaps that can create an administrative burden, ultimately resulting in missed, important security updates.

Poor data collection impacts effectiveness

Challenges in collecting and presenting actionable data from application owners makes it difficult to effectively measure and confirm the program’s value.

Lack of participation limits application owner efficacy

Application ownership is just one of many tasks employees juggle, making it easy for it to be overlooked. Regular reminders and tracking are essential to ensure actions are performed at the intervals dictated by the program.

General FAQs

Can App Owner help me set expectations for application owners?

Yes, App Owner is specifically designed to help set and manage expectations for application owners. It can communicate an editable overview of responsibilities, provides a simple form to complete when checking in on an application, and sends reminders to help ensure they do so on a defined schedule. This structured approach reduces ambiguity, improves accountability, and ensures that application owners stay well-informed and aligned with program goals.

How will App Owner make my application owner program successful?

App Onwer can make your application owner more successful in multiple ways:

Visibility in application owner programs is something that enterprises struggle with, especially with outdated or missing owner assignments. App Owner solves this by providing transparency on ownership, giving admins, IT managers, and owners clear insights, making the program easier to manage and sustain.

Governance is another pain point, with enterprises often taking a reactive stance on updates, leading to overwhelming backlogs. App Owner fosters a proactive approach by encouraging regular update checks and keeping applications secure and compliant while preventing delays and bottlenecks.

Measurability is key to success. App Owner offers metrics like application coverage, compliance, and a view of update recommendations, providing a clear snapshot of your application owner program. These metrics highlight improvement areas and track progress over time, fostering continuous optimization.

What helpful information can App Owner provide?

Juriba App Owner can provide you with helpful information such as:

Application Coverage: Reveals which applications have assigned owners and identifies any gaps. It ensures accountability by highlighting applications without owners, allowing for prompt assignment and improved management of the entire portfolio.

Compliance: Presents whether application owners are meeting their responsibilities, such as monitoring updates and security checks. Non-compliance is flagged, enabling swift corrective actions and ensuring adherence to organizational standards, thus reducing risk and enhancing performance.

Pending Updates: Tracks outstanding updates and deferred updates, highlighting overdue applications. By monitoring Pending Updates, you can address critical patches promptly, minimize security vulnerabilities, and maintain the overall health of your application portfolio.

What are the requirements for App Owner?

The requirements for App Owner are minimal, making it simple and quick to get started. As a native cloud-based Software as a Service (SaaS) application, all you need is an internet connection and a computer to access it. Once you're connected, you can easily import your applications and their corresponding owners into the system. The setup process is streamlined, allowing you to begin using App Owner within just a few minutes, without the need for complex installations or infrastructure. This ease of use ensures that enterprises can quickly adopt the tool and start managing their application owner programs efficiently.

Can Juriba App Owner automate the update process for applications?

Juriba App Owner doesn't directly automate the update process for applications, but it plays a key role in ensuring updates are tracked and flagged for attention. For automation, App Owner integrates with tools like Juriba App Readiness, which handles the packaging and testing of applications. This integration helps streamline the update process by reducing the workload on packaging teams, making the overall update cycle more efficient and less resource-intensive. Through this collaboration, enterprises can ensure timely updates while minimizing manual intervention.

Pricing FAQs

Why is App Owner so inexpensive?

App Owner is a specialized tool designed to tackle the challenges enterprises face when implementing their application owner programs. Unlike other workflow management tools, which demand extensive customizations to create workflows and processes, App Owner offers ready-to-use and simple-to-understand features that streamline and enhance the success of application owner programs.

We want to help as many customers as possible realize a successful application owner program, so we have endeavored to ensure pricing is not a factor when considering App Owner. For most, even pulling free tools together in an attempt to produce something similar, would cost more in terms of time and effort than simply subscribing to App Owner.

How is the subscription cost calculated?

The subscription cost is determined by the number of devices managed through App Owner:

  • For up to 3,000 devices, the cost is $3,000 annually (minimum charge).
  • For 3,001 to 16,000 devices, the cost is $1 per device annually.
  • For more than 16,000 devices, the cost is capped at $16,000 annually (maximum charge).

 

Are there any additional charges apart from the subscription cost?

No, the subscription cost covers all features included in App Owner. However, if you require integrations with our other products or additional services outside the standard offering, those may incur extra charges. Contact our sales team for tailored requirements.

What happens if the number of devices changes mid-subscription?

If the number of devices increases or decreases during the subscription period, you can modify your subscription by submitting a request. The subscription cost will be adjusted based on the updated number of devices, effective from the modification date.

Can I cancel my subscription? What happens to my data?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. Upon cancellation:

  • Access to App Owner will remain available until the end of the subscription term.
  • Your data will be retained for 90 days after the subscription ends to allow for renewal if you change your mind.
  • After this period, data will be permanently deleted in accordance with our data retention policy.

What is the renewal policy, or how can I renew my subscription?

You can submit a renewal request toward the end of your subscription term via the "Request for Renewal" form. Additionally, one of our sales team members will proactively reach out to you approximately one month before your subscription is due for renewal to ensure uninterrupted access to App Owner.
