Dave Steadman

Business Development Manager

I am the Business Development Manager for Aztec Support, playing a key role in driving business growth while upholding the core values that define Aztec. With a strong background in business development and long-term client relationship building, my role centres around networking, creating opportunities, meeting clients face-to-face, delivering tailored solutions, and ensuring every partnership reflects the quality and reliability Aztec Support is known for.

With over 20 years of experience in new business sales and account management, I believe success is achieved when people work together as one. For me, communication is everything. Taking the time to truly listen, understand each client’s challenges, and tailor solutions to meet their individual needs is the only way to begin building long-term relationships.

In my personal life, I’m that sports guy; anything to do with sports grabs my interest. During the summer months, you’ll usually find me playing cricket in the Portsmouth leagues or out on the golf course. In winter, I’m more likely to have my feet up, watching football.

I would be delighted to have a chat and get to know you if you are considering Aztec to be your trusted technology partner. Check my schedule here and book a call or Teams meeting with me. I look forward to speaking with you!
