Enhance Efficiency with IT Infrastructure Management

Don’t let your networks and other critical IT infrastructure go unmanaged

We can manage your critical infrastructure and networks. Networks can be complex or simple in their design depending on the size of your business, the number of devices you have connected and other factors such as internet connectivity.

A poorly maintained network can lead to costly unplanned downtime. Nearly all of the devices in your business are connected to one another or through central switches. If a switch breaks or has a fault, you may not be able to access client files, or email, or the internet.

Don’t expose your central IT infrastructure to risk by ignoring warning signs or failing to perform maintenance and security tasks. Let us take care of your systems to ensure business productivity with our technical IT infrastructure support.

Reliable IT Backup Plans

Data is the lifeblood of your business

Data can be lost through a systems failure like a server or computer crash, accidental deletion, a cyber attack or other malicious activity. Without your data, you may not have a business to run at all. Having a robust managed back up plan in place is critical.

Aztec’s IT support consulting reviews your current backup plan, make suggestions for improvements and provide a range of backup plans and services that will ensure your business data is securely protected.

In many instances utilising cloud backup with automation, encryption and stored in UK data centres is a cost-effective way to achieve peace of mind for your business data.

Protection from IT Disasters

Frequently Asked Questions About Managed IT Services

What are managed IT services?

Managed IT is handled by a third party managed service company who will look after a clients IT needs. These services can help you save time and improve efficiencies in-house and will usually be available at a fixed rate cost. A managed service provider will take a proactive approach to monitoring and managing your IT infrastructure.

What are the benefits of managed IT services?

Using a managed service provider offers many benefits. Qualified experts can help you identify problems quickly and react to any issues, minimise any potential downtime, save business costs from hiring internal teams while you also benefit from predictable pricing, flexible and reliable support, staying compliant and reducing cyber security risks.

What is included in a managed IT service?

Depending on the managed it service provider, a wide range of services may be offered including network testing, device monitoring, disaster recovery, data and server backup plans and system performance monitoring.

At Aztec Support, our managed IT support services include scheduled maintenance services, daily checks on security and backups and proactive user support.
