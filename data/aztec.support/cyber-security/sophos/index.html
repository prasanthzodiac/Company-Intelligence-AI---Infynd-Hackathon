Sophos EndPoint Intercept X Advanced Protection

Sophos offer sophisticated endpoint protection against the latest threats for computers, laptops, mobile devices and servers. At a minimum, Sophos Endpoint Intercept X Advanced is recommended to all clients by Aztec.

Sophos Endpoint Intercept X protects your IT system from advanced threats in the following ways:

  • Anti-Ransomware
  • Exploit Prevention
  • Deep Learning Technology
  • Central Management
  • Active Adversary Mitigations
  • Zero Trust Network Access

EDR (Extended Detect and Response) builds on the Intercept X and adds machine learning to provide a 24/7 active response to threats. EDR will isolate a device and take automated steps to triage the attack immediately.

 

MTR (Managed Threat Response) takes this even further with 24/7 human threat hunters overseen by Sophos SOC (Security Operations Centre) analysts, who have greater autonomy over actions they can take to mitigate and stop an attack, including forensic snapshots.

Protect Sensitive Data With Sophos Encryption

Sophos offers managed hard drive encryption to mitigate data loss and protect against GDPR issues from lost or stolen devices. A managed encryption service ensures that recovery keys are securely stored if an encryption key is lost or forgotten.

Phishing Awareness from Sophos

Can your team reliably spot a phishing email attempting to harvest credentials? We can phish your team and report back with who opened the email, who clicked the phishing link, who entered creds and back this up with reporting to business owners and training material for all users who need the extra help in identifying a phishing email.

Strengthen Network Security With Sophos Next Gen Firewall & VPN Services

Ordinary ‘BT’ Hubs are inadequate for today’s cyber intrusion threats. The perimeter of your physical network is under constant bombardment. A standalone BT hub is unaware of the threat status of the devices behind it; event logging is minimal, and advanced security controls are limited or non-existent.

A Sophos Next-Gen firewall is in constant sync with the computers and servers it’s protecting. It can isolate a device from others on the network and prevent it from accessing external resources while allowing other devices to operate normally. Advanced Sophos security features such as DDoS protection, and Country and Geographic IP blocking further reduces the attack surface. Using a VPN via Sophos Firewall provides a secure way for remote workers to access company resources and peace of mind for business owners.

Enhance Cloud Security with Sophos Cloud Optix

Sophos offer a broad range of protection for cloud-based (Azure and AWS) servers and resources. Cloud Optix provides real-time monitoring of cloud environments along with compliance and vulnerability reporting and management.

Unified Security Management with Sophos Central Orchestration

At the heart of Sophos solutions, Sophos Central offers synchronised security across all devices and services, monitoring security health status, and using AI to provide a holistic approach to perimeter, cloud and endpoint security.
