MyGlue Business Password Management & Document Collaboration

Protect Your Business from Cyber Threats with MyGlue

Did you know 70% of cyberattacks target small to medium-sized businesses? A data breach can cost your business in downtime, reputation, and finances.

Don’t wait until it’s too late. MyGlue helps safeguard your sensitive data, ensuring your business stays secure, efficient, and operational—without the hassle.

Request more information

Password management for your business

As technologies evolve and the level of cyber security measures needed to protect your business increases, it’s likely that your business will have hundreds of different passwords to access all your day-to-day tools and platforms. Managing a vast range of secure passwords can be impractical and inefficient, and may even cause issues such as data breaches and losses of valuable information if left to manage manually.

This is where a business password manager comes in. MyGlue is an efficient and secure way to create, store and autofill passwords when required. They help to maintain the highest level of password security allowing your business to securely manage its sensitive information.

Why use MyGlue?

MyGlue allows your business to centrally store, share and access passwords in a simple, yet secure platform. Your team members will also be able to add their own personal passwords for their own viewing which only they will have access to. You’ll no longer need to share passwords through unsecure emails, you can simply access the password you require on your MyGlue account or MyGlue web app. 

Other benefits of MyGlue business password manager

1. Take control of password security with 3 tiers of user permissions

Control access with three user permission tiers—‘read only,’ ‘read and add,’ or ‘read and remove.’ MyGlue’s autofill feature makes logging in seamless via browser extension or mobile app. Plus, your data stays live and up to date, eliminating outdated passwords.

2. Ensure privacy & security at all times

Enhance security with fingerprint authentication for extra protection. MyGlue generates strong, secure passwords, eliminating the risks of weak, easy-to-guess logins. If a master password is deleted, your provider can restore access without viewing sensitive details.

3. Instant access to essential business data

Whether your team is working remotely or on the go, MyGlue provides instant access to essential business data via the mobile app. Stay protected from cyber threats and streamline password management effortlessly.

How MyGlue Simplifies Password Management

MyGlue’s password management tool stores all of your passwords in one place, meaning you just have to search the name of the website you want to login to in MyGlue to find the username and password of the site. You can then directly access the site through MyGlue and copy the login details across to sign in. 

Control access with customizable visibility settings, ensuring only the right people can see sensitive credentials. MyGlue also tracks every login attempt, giving you a clear audit trail.

Lastly, MyGlue takes the hassle away of updating passwords with team members, as once a site’s login details are stored within MyGlue, you simply need to update the password within your MyGlue account and ensure the relevant team members can see this new password. This means no more having to hunt for new password request emails!

Document management for businesses

With the ongoing process of digitisation, the concept of a paperless office is becoming an everyday reality. More and more businesses expect to have access to the documents they need as soon as they need them. A document management system eases the document management process within your business through features such as collaboration tools, checklist tools and sharing systems.

A document management tool such as MyGlue will allow your business to improve its efficiency and productivity, therefore boosting business profits in the long run.

Why use MyGlue for document management?

MyGlue allows for all business documents to be securely stored, accessed and shared within your organisation. Gone are the days of spending time searching through folders or your email history. With MyGlue you can login to a secure digital interface and access the document you require with full information and version history.

Boost efficiency & stay secure with MyGlue

As well as exceptional password and document management tools, MyGlue offers several efficiency and security-boosting features for your business:

Collaboration tools

MyGlue is a collaborative tool, allowing you and your IT service provider to securely share information. This allows your IT provider to gain more visibility into your IT environment, helping them to deliver an even better, quality service.

Empower through self-service

Enhance the productivity of your team by empowering them to solve simple and repetitive issues by securely accessing SOPs shared by your service provider through MyGlue. Your IT provider will be able to securely share the necessary information and documents to help you to quickly troubleshoot any issues.

Airtight security

Benefit from airtight security with role-based permissions, audit trails, version history and two-factor authentication.

How it works

MyGlue works by allowing you to share documents and collaborate with your internal teams and external providers, whilst also securely holding all of your business’ passwords in an easy-to-use platform.
