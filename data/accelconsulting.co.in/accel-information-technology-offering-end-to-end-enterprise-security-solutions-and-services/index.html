Accel Information Technology: Offering End-to-end Enterprise Security Solutions and Services

09 Nov
Posted by: Accel Information Technology
Category: press interview - CIO review Magzine
No Comments

Enterprise security threats, over time, have continued to increase and attain a higher level of sophistication. Organizations are experiencing a significant increase in security incidents, cyber attacks, and data breaches. Additionally, with the onset of the Covid-19 pandemic, enterprise security leaders are now busier than ever. Organizations, small and large, need to revisit the state of their enterprise security, address the various challenges encountered and adopt security initiatives as organizations accommodate a gamut of advancing technologies and digital transformation initiatives.

However, most businesses are not well equipped to mitigate the risks and need an experienced enterprise security services and solution provider to employ a customer-centric approach that will help them better understand their unique pain points and leverage the right solution.

Perfectly poised to meet and exceed every customer’s varied enterprise security requirements is Accel Information Technology, a group of Accel Consulting, with over 20 years of in-depth experience. “We are a certified ISO 27001 lead implementer and having this background has helped our company focus on the Security segment in SMB & large enterprises by consulting and addressing cyber security, Phy security, Access control and software related security issues,” says Syed Saqueeb Ahamed, Director of Accel Information Technology. The company offers an array of IT enterprise services and solutions such as ISO 27001 (ISMS) implementation and Audit, Cyber security, Data Centers, Security related to Access controls and Web & Mobile application & Software.

Ensuring Customer Satisfaction
Commanding quite the market presence, the company was established in 2009 as Accel Consulting, located in Bangalore. The company then went on to grow into different divisions which are Accel Information Technology located in UAE, Accel Infotec located in UK and Accel Consulting. Each of these divisions has its own small business unit with a highly qualified team to ensure successful implementation and strong support.
These aforementioned divisions were allocated to cater to multiple segments of IT, such as Enterprise Security, Audits related to ISO 27001 and its implementation, IoT implementation, Robotic Process Automation, and Software divisions run by qualified Business unit managers. Once a customer is on-board, they are provided with a cost effective solution and best-in-class support.

 

The company has worked with a plethora of well renowned clients hailing from the Middle East markets and has been serving the Government Customers with three branches in UAE. “Most of our customer success stories come from the UAE government departments. Being a trusted Consultant and having won many certifications and appreciation speaks volumes about the trust the customers have in us,” adds Syed.
