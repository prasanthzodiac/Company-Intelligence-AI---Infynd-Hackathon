PCI Data Security Standard

 

Selecting a PCI Consultant
AC Assure has formally accredited Payment Card Industry Professionals (PCIP) staff who can assist with helping you achieve compliance to the PCI DSS standard. As Payment Card Industry Professionals they have all demonstrated there understanding to the PCI DSS. Although we have only recently started offering PCI DSS services we are not new to payment scheme audits, having provided both PCI Card Production audits and 3D Secure audits over the past 4 years. In fact a core element of vendors wishing to meet the Visa 3D Secure Requirements is first to be PCI DSS compliant. Having performed over 200 payment scheme audits we understand about the implementation of security in real world environments and our wealth of experience of allows us to offer a range of solutions to secure your systems. As a results we can offer a complete range of services to ensure Merchants and Service Providers meet Payment Card Industry Services (PCI-DSS) compliance. Our approach to each assignment can be adapted according to the size and specific requirements of our clients, we produce a personalised offering to meet with your individual needs.
PCI DSS Services
For Merchants and Service Providers, we provide a range of professional PCI services. Depending on the specific client requirements, this can include from engagement:
  • PCI DSS Readiness Assessment
  • PCI DSS Report on Compliance
  • PCI DSS Consulting
  • Penetration Testing
  • Vulnerability Scanning
  • PCI DSS Policy & Procedure Development

Need Help in Preparing for Your Compliance Audit?

  • Need Help in Preparing for Your Compliance Audit?

    We provide pre audit assessments to assist vendors in their preparation for future compliance audits inline with the security standards from the two leading card payment brands.

  • Need Help Following Your Compliance Audit?

    We provide vendors with assistance for remediation of non compliance issues raised during audit which require corrective action.
