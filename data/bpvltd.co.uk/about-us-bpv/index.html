About Bio Products Validation | BPV Ltd

Our History

Bio Products Validation Ltd (BPV) was established to offer the best level of validation services within the UK and Ireland life science industry. With a dedicated team of validation engineers and consultants, BPV’s team has provided services to the life science industry since 2005.

We cover the whole equipment lifecycle, including Installation Qualification (IQ), Operational Qualification (OQ), Performance Qualification (PQ), development testing, requalification and book-end validation.

Our Experience

As a company, our engineers have over 100 years of combined experience working on a variety of validation projects, small and large, from ad-hoc jobs qualifying equipment to large-scale projects where many engineers are needed over the long term. Every company’s needs are different, so we will work with you to achieve your desired goals and outcomes efficiently. We cover all areas of validation from thermal validation to CSV, so whatever your project needs, we can cater to you.
