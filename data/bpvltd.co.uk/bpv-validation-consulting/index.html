VALIDATION CONSULTING SERVICES

Your validation consultants

At BPV, validation is our niche. Our Subject Matter Experts (SMEs) have unparalleled knowledge and experience in providing validation consulting services, setting us apart from other firms, such as recruiters or agencies.

Whether you require assistance with validation master plans, validation protocols, or any other aspect of the validation process, our team is ready to help your company achieve its goals while maintaining regulatory compliance.

Our validation consulting services extend beyond VMPs and protocol development. We offer project management support, from project initiation to production, validation planning, remediation, and optimisation, as well as deviation management and closure. Our consultants are well-versed in validation methods and processes according to regulatory requirements, ensuring that our clients’ operations are fully compliant.

As your validation consultant, we help life sciences companies navigate the complex process of equipment and system validation. Our team of Subject Matter Experts has extensive knowledge and experience in providing Validation Master Plans (VMPs), computer systems validation, equipment qualifications, process validation, and test method validation services.

At the core of our Validation Consulting services is the development of robust Validation Master Plans (VMPs) and validation protocols. BPV has its own validation protocols and clients can follow our cGMP-compliant documentation or can choose to use their own. Our consultants work closely with clients to understand their specific requirements and develop tailored VMPs that outline the planned validation activities, essential documents, staff responsibilities, and a documented approach for conducting Installation Qualification (IQ), Operational Qualification (OQ), and Performance Qualification (PQ) in compliance with cGMP requirements.

The VMPs created by our validation consultants employ risk analysis to establish the scope and extent of qualification protocols, applicable test methods, critical control points requiring evaluation, sampling locations, frequency, and size, as well as schedules for requalification. Key documents included in the VMPs are user requirement specifications, impact assessments, risk analyses, traceability matrices, qualification plans and reports, deviation reports, and final reports.

In addition to Validation Master Plan services, BPV offers assistance with validation throughout the entire lifecycle of equipment and systems. Our full validation package can be customised to suit client needs and typically includes Design Qualification (DQ), Installation Qualification (IQ), Operational Qualification (OQ), Performance Qualification (PQ), Equipment Specific Development Testing, and Requalification packages.

What is Validation Consultancy?

Validation consultancy provides organisations with professional expertise and guidance and support, especially in highly regulated industries, helping them to manufacture high quality products whilst also meeting the relevant regulatory standards and industry best practices for the validation process.

Validation consultancy services typically cover all stages of the validation process, from process validation and equipment validation to cleaning validation, method validation, and computer system validation.

To take advantage of our team’s expertise in validation consultancy, reach out to us today to discuss your needs or request a quote.

Benefits of Hiring a Validation Consultant

Regulatory Compliance

Validation consultancy ensures your systems, processes, and equipment all comply with every relevant UK and international regulatory requirement.

Quality and Safety

Validation ensures your processes are consistent and repeatable and lead to the production of high quality, safe, and effective products.

Cost Effectiveness

By preventing compliance failures and recalls, validation consultancy ultimately helps your business to time and money in the long term.

Expert Guidance

Validation consultancy gives you access to specialised expertise and best practices, helping you to navigate complex validation processes with confidence.
