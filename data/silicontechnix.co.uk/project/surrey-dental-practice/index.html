Surrey Dental Practice

Surrey Dental Practice is a modern dental practice based in Guildford. They have a team of friendly dentists dedicated to providing you with the highest quality, personalized dental services at affordable prices.

They are experienced in all aspects of dentistry including General Dentistry, Cosmetic Dentistry, Smile Makeovers, and Veneers. They are able to provide a One-Stop Service including dental implants, endodontic/root canal treatment, orthodontics (Inman and clear aligners) as well as hygienist services.

Project Details

At Silicon Technix, we created a website for Surrey Dental Practice that features a user-friendly interface, high-quality images, and detailed information about their staff, services, and treatment options. The menu bar provides easy access to pages such as About Us, Services, free guide , and Contact Us, while the Services page includes detailed information about general dentistry, cosmetic dentistry, and orthodontics.

The website also has a free guide page with NHS Price List
And Private Price. The Contact Us page includes a contact form, phone number, and email address. The new website features a modern, responsive design that is easy to navigate and optimized for all devices.

Overall, our team at Silicon Technix was proud to deliver a high-quality website that met the client’s requirements and exceeded their expectations. The new website provides a seamless user experience, while also showcasing the practice’s expertise and services. We are confident that the new website will help Surrey Dental Practice attract new patients and grow their business.

We follow the guidelines and Standards set by GDC, CQC, BDA, NHS, and ICO, ensuring your practice operates professionally and in full compliance with all regulations.
