Audio-Visual Tape Specialists | Digitising Archives for Future Preservation

Oxford Duplication specialise in the preservation of analog video, audio and motion film archives for historical libraries, companies, governments and institutions.  Pleave view below for a comprehensive list of format that we can convert.

Our Audio-Visual studio boasts an impressive collection of professional AV broadcast machines for the conversion of all broadcast tapes.  We are proud to be the only leading digitisation company in the UK and Europe offering cutting edge quality conversions using World leading AWS Elemental archival analogue conversion units, alongside Blackmagic decks, Prism Sound and Izotope audio restoration.  Our injestion systems produce the highest quality archival (AVI/FFV1) and Pro-Res outputs between all video standards, frame rates and resolutions, catering for bulk or small order projects, providing output options to virtually any file formats.
