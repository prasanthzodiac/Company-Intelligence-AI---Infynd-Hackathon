Ad-Hoc One-Off Shredding Services in Oxford UK  

Oxford Duplication Centre provide ad-hoc and one-off shredding services in Oxford for companies and private clients who do not require a regular collection service. These services are perfect if you are moving offices and clearing out documentation that is no longer required.  Or for consumer clients working through old archive documents that are not required at the end of each year.

Our services are award winning.  With our one-off shredding service you can bring your documents in during our office hours and leave them with our team.    Once your documents are shredded, we will issue you with our 'proof of shredding' certificate for your digital records. 

CERTIFICATE OF REGISTRATION NO: CBDU540973
All shredding is prepared at our offices with a certificate of completion.  With the law it states through The Data Protection Act 1998 that all materials relating to individuals, employees, customers and company information such as suppliers accounts must be disposed of in a secure manner. We can ensure maximum protection with our on-site distruction services.
