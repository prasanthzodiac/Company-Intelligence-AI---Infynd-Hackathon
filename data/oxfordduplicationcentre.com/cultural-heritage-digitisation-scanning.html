Benefits of Heritage Digitisation by Preserving through Scanning

The digitisation of heritage and historical archives ensures that not only the past is preserved but that the media presented for media can be retained, preserved and viewed with mimimum damage. 

  •  Digitisation is important to convert physical collections into digital formats, enabling them to be accessible to a wider audience who can explore and connect with the past anywhere throughout the world.  
  •  Creating digital copies, especially fragile items will mitigate the risk of damage from handling and environmental factors
  •  In the tragic event of a disaster, such as fire or flood, digital copies can provide an invaluable backup.
  • Non-Destructive Book Scanning
  • 78's, BBC Transcription Discs and Vinyls 
  • Contactless Flatbed Scanning upto A0+ 

 

Our Heritage Digitisation Services

Document and Manuscript Digitisation

Our specialist document and manuscript digitisation service digitises historical documents into National Archive Standard digital formats. Perfect for the preservation of parchments, registers, manuscripts, letters, and archival papers, easily making them accessible for online viewing.

Books, Diaries, Newspapers, Property Deeds

Our flatbed scanning machinery can carefully digitise fragile books, printed documents, war diaries, old newspapers, parchment curled or straight and deed packs. Media is carefully relaxed and digitised to produce an exact digital replica of the image.  Using National Archive Standards, you can trust our expertise in preparing the highest quality digital outputs required for future preservation. 

Microfilms, Slides, Negatives, and Glass-Plates

Our award-winning photographic film digitisation process for all film types including medium format, transparencies, 35mm, aperture cards, microfilms, glass plates and specialist film types is always an extemely popular department.  Our archive technicians use specialist scanners capable of digitising imagery in the highest quality RAW or TIFF outputs.   

Photographs and Ephemera

With fragile soft handling bulk digtiisation machinery, we can digitise fragile photographs and ephemera with ease, allowing efficient and careful preservation of historical media.

Digitising Documents

Working with clients all over the UK, we digitise corporate, consumer and heritage document, book and map archives into suitable digital files for online use. 

Our Bookeye A1 Book Scanner offers high resolution planetary scanning for fragile delicate books and rare maps, parchment or materials  up to DIN A1+.   

Digitising Photo Film

Our archive department is equipped with the leading photographic film and photograph machinery, ensuring consistent and high quality digitistion of all film type formats from 110mm to glass plate negatives.

Adhering to The National Archives Specifications, our  services are both leading and award winning, ensuring complete confidence. 

Digitising Audio-Video 

Working with the leading AWS and Blackmagic conversion processors, our services incorporate every audio, video, vinyl and reel format in both open spool and closed tape formats. 

Digital file formats can support clients with Pro-ResHQ, 10-bit AVI,  MPEG4, AIFF, WAV, MP3, with most other options available upon request.
