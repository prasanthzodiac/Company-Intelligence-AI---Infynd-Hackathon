Oxford Duplication are a multi-award winning digitisation specialising in the preservation of discontinued magnetic video tape and audio tape media,  photographic film types, x-ray film types and all format document scanning digitisation services to A0+.  

Supporting corporate, heritage and consumer collections and valuable archives.  Our offices and studios are based just outside the beautiful city of Oxford, And with a fully equipped  studio, we can provide a complete digitisation service for clients.   Our company hold an impressive client base, we are one of the leading experts in digital conversions within the UK.
