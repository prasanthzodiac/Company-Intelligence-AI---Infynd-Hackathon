Reprographic & WideTek Artwork Scanning Systems  | Digitisation Services

motion & cine film | audio | video | optical |  photographic film | books | documents  | Scan on Demand | FAQs

Our Reprographic Art Scanning and WideTek A0+ Studio systems are designed as a versatile high-performance solution which opens up an extraordinary range of possibilities for digital archiving. It is the ideal solution for demanding digitisation projects in the field of cultural heritage that involve special requirements in terms of format, surface structure and illumination: Whether stamps or large formats, coins or paintings, sealed letters or glass negatives, loose, bound, flat, raised or corrugated originals, glossy or matt surfaces or a wide variety of material structures, incident , transmitted or raking light.  

Digitisation and Archiving Specialists: Family Archives (paper, audio-video, cine and photographs) | Government and Corporate Documents | Photographic Film Materials | Paper & Parchment | Books & Scrapbooks | Digital Media | Broadcast Audio, Video & Motion Picture Film

Our experienced technicians ensure a 'once only digital scan'. Our non-destructive scanning service is ideal for rare, precious, and valuable media upto A0 in size . Using state-of-the-art FUDGI national archive standards for all digitisation our services can also be used for GIglee images, supporting ArtsWeek and artists in Oxfordshire and the surrounding areas. Utilising the overhead scanners with archival book-cradles, books are digitised  intact without the worry of bindings becoming untact or damaged. With specialist software media are scanned 24-bit or 16-bit . Digital output include Archival TIFF/RAW, JPEG, PDF (PDFA) and PDF OCR (searchable content), multipage PDFs, WORD or eBooks. Delivery can be via hard drive, electronic download or secure FTP.
