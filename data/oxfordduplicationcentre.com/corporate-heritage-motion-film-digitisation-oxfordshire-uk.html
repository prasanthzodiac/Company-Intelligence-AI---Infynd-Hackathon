Corporate and Heritage 9.5mm, 8mm, 16mm and 35mm Cine and Motion Film Scanning Solutions | Oxford UK

Oxford Duplication specialise in the preservation of analog video, audio and motion film archives for historical libraries, companies, governments and institutions.   We offer many output options from Native, Pro-Res, MOV, Blu-Ray and DVD for all cine and motion film, optical sound, magnetic sound.  Our services are highly valued for the quality and preservation of precious media.
