Our Partners

Our Partners

We collaborate with a number of amazing companies that help us deliver an outstanding product and service.

These include the following organisations:

iomart

Hosting Partner

iomart are our hosting partner and provide us with a secure and resilient UK-based infrastructure to support our software and business, giving clients peace of mind.

They provide an outstanding service and hold various security certifications and are publicly listed on the Alternative Investment Market of the London Stock Exchange.

We have been working with iomart since we formed in 2008 and their infrastructure continually evolves to meet the demands of our ever-expanding range of clients.

Find Out More

Secarma

Security Partner

At Creatio the security of our system and the data stored within it is at the heart of our design and delivery approach; and Secarma plays a role in this!

Secarma are a cybersecurity consulting company that utilises ethical hacking methods to test the strength of our company’s existing security defences.

Their amazing team undertake an independent penetration test of our system every year to provide us and our clients with as much assurance as possible regarding the strength of our cybersecurity.

They are incredibly helpful and provide informed feedback and suggestions where needed.

Find Out More

British Standards Institution (BSI)

We are delighted to use the British Standards Institution (BSI) as our external ISO auditors for our ISO certifications (ISO 22301 Business Continuity System Management and ISO 27001 Information Security, Cybersecurity and Privacy Protection - Information Security Management System).

Their rigorous, comprehensive approach and insight has helped us to continually enhance our processes and systems to meet the various ISO requirements.

We truly value their robust scrutiny and we have been working with them since 2017. Therefore, we have no hesitation in recommending their services to others looking to seek external certification against international standards.

Find Out More

Federation of
Awarding Bodies

We are proud of our history in the Awarding Organisation (AO) sector and have been a member of the representative body, the Federation of Awarding Bodies (FAB), since 2009.

With an amazing, proactive team, FAB provide a fantastic service to their members within the sector.

Their annual conference is incredible, and we never miss the opportunity to attend and network within the industry and interact with our existing customers.

If you’re new to the regulated AO or EPAO sectors we would encourage you to contact them to see how they may be able to help you.
