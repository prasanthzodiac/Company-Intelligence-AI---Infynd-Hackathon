An overview of creatiogreen

creatiogreen is the name of our powerful and flexible Software as a Service (SaaS) solution designed for organisations who develop, deliver and award qualification products across various industries. Providing a single solution capable of supporting numerous business processes, with three different configurable entry points for your staff, customers and learners and a variety of set features.

Each client is empowered to configure the system URL, brand and scope to suit their needs. Depending on their requirements, an organisation can deploy individual or all of the available six modules:

  • Product Development

  • Customer Management

  • Register, Assess & Award

  • Governance

  • End-Point Assessments

  • Learner Portal

Each module comes pre-loaded with templates, field and label settings aligned to reflect typical education workflows and regulatory requirements to help speed up your configuration. creatiogreen offers the ability to configure these modules along with other features of the solution to align with your arrangements through the utilisation of our powerful and intuitive configuration tool.

Regardless of your configuration, creatiogreen will work on any internet-enabled device and does not require you, your staff or customers to download, install or maintain any software. It is fully hosted and managed by us, removing client concerns about the infrastructure, upgrades and scalability of the solution- allowing organisations to benefit from the arrangements we have put in place.

You simply decide the scope in terms of modules and the features you wish to deploy.

The system is qualification and product neutral, and its flexibility means it is used by a diverse range of organisations which span a wide range of sectors and countries. Some of these sectors include:

Accounting & Finance | Agriculture & Horticulture | Building & Construction | Business Administration & Management |

Childcare | Coaching | Community Work | Counselling | Creative Arts & Design | Education | Engineering | Export & Trade |

Fitness | Food & Drink | Hair & Beauty | Health & Social Care | Hospitality | IT | Journalism | Law | Leadership & Management |

Logistics | Manufacturing | Media & Marketing | Medical & Dentistry | Motor | Oil & Gas | Preparation for Life & Work |

Professional Services | Public Health | Rail | Renewables | Retail | Safety | Social Care | Sports & Leisure | Veterinary

creatiogreen continually evolves throughout the year, developing and deploying new innovative features that truly enhance our clients operations and services. We love to innovate and truly transform!

Modules

The creatiogreen system is qualification and product neutral, and seamlessly connects a wide range of associated processes through its six integrate modules shown below.

Product Development

The Product Development module transforms how you develop, launch, review and withdraw various qualification products.

It strengthens your internal control, transparency, and compliance with relevant regulatory requirements, supporting virtually any qualification type, assessment and grading model.

Data entered in this module underpins other modules and processes and can be integrated with external systems such as your company website or exam platforms. It also supports the production of delivery materials, allowing you to enter data once and use it across different activities, reducing data duplication, burden, and the risk of errors.

Find Out More

Customer Management

The Customer Management module streamlines and enhances customer interactions, including approval applications, quality assurance, investigations, sampling activities, enquiries, requests, complaints and appeals.

It supports risk rating, action assignment, customer sanctions, and the management of your quality assurance staff. Additionally, it transforms how you provide information and updates to customers.

This module is proven to reduce turnaround times, strengthen quality assurance and delivery arrangements, offering a clear and intuitive CRM profile for each customer and activity.

Find Out More

Register, Assess & Award

The Register, Assess & Award module supports an organisation’s end-to-end learner registration, assessment delivery, and certification processes, allowing you to enforce and apply different delivery and awarding rules for various product types.

Through a seamless integration with the Customer Management module, it can transform the way you sample and moderate assessment decisions.

Whilst its integration with the grading and awarding features in the Product Development module, will enable you to automate, where relevant, the awarding and certifications activities. Therefore, significantly streamlining processes for staff and customers, saving valuable time and money.

Find Out More

Governance

The Governance module will transform and strengthen your governance arrangements, covering risk management, incident management, self-assessments, and compliance with regulatory requirements such as the General Conditions, SQA Regulatory Principles and external certification schemes like ISO 9001, ISO 22301, ISO 27001, Cyber Essentials and Cyber Essentials Plus.

The module truly streamlines and enhances governance processes and can be integrated with the Product Development, Customer Management and EPAO modules, seamlessly bringing operational and governance information together.

Find Out More

Learner Portal

The Learner Portal enables direct engagement for organisations with their learners, whether they are registered via their customers or registered by the learner themselves, directly from your website.

The portal enables learners to view resources, available products, membership programmes and associated information and register for qualifications and exams - making direct payments if required.

Learners can manage their learning journeys, engage directly with your organisation, and access their results and certificates. They can even share their achievements with external parties if they wish.

Find Out More

End-Point Assessments

The End-Point Assessments (EPA) module underpins all end-point assessment organisation (EPAO) activities, from setting up different apprenticeship standards and associated delivery, gateway, EPA requests, and assessor allocation arrangements, to establishing associated assessment and awarding rules.

The module also helps demonstrate compliance with each associated EPA General Condition and includes a wide range of regulatory aligned reports and features.

This module seamlessly integrates with other creatiogreen modules, providing a single-entry point for your customers and staff across your entire product range - eliminating the need to implement and pay for multiple systems and integrations.
