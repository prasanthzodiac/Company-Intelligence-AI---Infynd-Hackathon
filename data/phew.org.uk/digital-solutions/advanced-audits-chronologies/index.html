How It Works

Step 1: Enhanced Setup

Configure your audit with case-level security protocols and rapid deployment templates. Set up multi-agency access with appropriate permissions and information governance controls. 

Step 2: Automated Distribution & Tracking

Send audits to all relevant agencies simultaneously. The system automatically tracks responses, sends intelligent reminders, and monitors completion status across all participants. 

Step 3: Live Data Collection

Agencies can update information continuously throughout the audit period. All responses are automatically collated, secured at case level, and integrated into the evolving case picture. 

Step 4: Automated Chronology Creation

The system builds comprehensive chronologies automatically from all collected data, creating detailed, court-ready timelines that meet legal and compliance standards. 

Step 5: Report-Ready Output

Export structured, report-ready data with integrated chronologies for final case reports, ensuring all information is organised and accessible for decision-making.
