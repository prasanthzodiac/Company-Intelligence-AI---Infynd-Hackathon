Our standards

S8080 have invested heavily in making our agency the best it can possibly be. We believe that the standards we have achieved help give you confidence when dealing with us.

ISO 27001

ISO/IEC 27001:2022 information security standard for the protection of client and company sensitive data (ISO 27001 certified since 2006).

Cyber Essentials Plus

S8080 achieved the Cyber Essentials Plus Information Security certification in December 2017.

Cyber Essentials

S8080 achieved the Cyber Essentials Information Security certification in December 2017.

SC (Security Check) Clearance 

Due to the nature of our work all s8080 developers, technical architects and head of operations have SC (Security Check) clearance for government.

Quality, Environmental, Health & Safety and Social Value

  • Quality Management System - ISO 9001 quality standard (ISO 9001 since 2006)
  • Environmental Management System – ISO 14001 (ISO 9001 since 2007)
  • Occupational Health and Safety Management System – ISO 45001 (ISO 9001 since 2007)
  • Social Value Framework - ISO 26000 (Since 2025)

Carbon neutral

At S8080 we have worked hard since 2015 to reduce out impact on the environment, increase sustainability and reduce our carbon footprint. Over the years we have taken significant steps to reduce our emissions,  and our Carbon Reduction Plan sets out real, measurable steps to cut emissions further to reach Net Zero by 2040.  

We have for a long time believed that offsetting via high quality verified projects was what we could also do to help offset our impact, including UK tree planting schemes since 2022 with Carbon Neutral Britain.

Sustainability

We use services from supplier with sustainability in mind to reduce our environmental impact and support responsible digital practices. For example, we host our websites using Microsoft Azure, which is committed to sustainability through various initiatives, including:

  • 100% renewable energy powering all Azure datacentres by 2025

  • Carbon negative commitment by 2030, aiming to remove more carbon than they emit

  • Water positive operations by 2030, meaning they will replenish more water than they consume

  • Zero waste goal by 2030, including reuse, repurposing, and recycling of hardware

and Zoho for our business processes who has made strong commitments to environmental responsibility through initiatives such as:

  • Solar power at data centres and campuses

  • Green, energy-efficient data centres

  • Water conservation & zero-waste policies,

  • Support for remote work & green mobility

By choosing partners like Azure and Zoho for our key business practices, we align our digital infrastructure with our environmental values.
