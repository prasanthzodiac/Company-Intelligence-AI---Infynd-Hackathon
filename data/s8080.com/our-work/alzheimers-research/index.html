Alzheimer's Research UK - blogging platform

s8080_user - Thu, 18/05/2017

We were thrilled to take on the task of developing a new WordPress blogging platform for Alzheimer's Research UK, a cause that holds great significance for Matt and our team. The primary objective was to create an impactful website that raises awareness about dementia and showcases the invaluable work carried out by Alzheimer's Research UK as a leading charity in funding dementia research. The blog section serves as a platform for staff members to share their expertise and for distinguished guest bloggers, including Sir Terry Pratchett, to engage in meaningful discussions about various dementia-related topics. Our focus was on delivering an exceptional user experience, which involved incorporating features such as comments, seamless responsiveness across devices, integration with donation links, and robust social connections. From providing UX advice and developing information architecture to designing an adaptable layout for different devices, setting up the WordPress content management system, and optimizing server resources for optimal performance, our comprehensive services ensured the successful realization of the project.

Our collaboration with Alzheimer's Research UK allowed us to create a powerful WordPress blogging platform that effectively communicates their mission and engages a broader audience in the fight against dementia. We take immense pride in contributing our expertise to this vital project, which aims to make a positive impact in the lives of those affected by dementia.
