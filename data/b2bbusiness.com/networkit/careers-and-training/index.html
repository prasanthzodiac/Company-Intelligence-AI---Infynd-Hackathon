Careers & Training

Here at B2B Business Technology Group we’re fortunate enough to be growing rapidly as a business, with an ever expanding list of clients. And we are always looking for talented new people who want to join our team.

Engage and
succeed with
B2B
Business Technology group

Since our foundation in 2008, we’ve maintained a strong culture that encourages staff to GROW and live our important values to set goals and find solutions to problems.

Working at B2B Business Technology Group is not just about technical knowledge. Being part of the team is a great opportunity for you to develop and grow in a wide range of roles. There’s plenty of scope for making a real contribution to the future of the company too.

We continually support and invest in our staff through on-going training and industry-related accreditations. We deliver staff engagement initiatives including career development and employee reward and recognition programmes.

Our employees are always gaining new skills and progressing within, which contributes to our extremely low staff turnover.
