About

Jumany.uk is a project born and created as a turning point in the broader and more chaotic schedule of hosting houses and providers in general. An idea carried out by a team of Italian professionals with twenty years of experience in the sector, with headquarters in the United Kingdom, London Legal Headquarters and Cardiff Operational Headquarters and Data Centers in London, Manchester, Singapore, USA and Italy with all the guarantees and attentions due to a project that has an evident international vocation.

Jumany.uk, multilingual (English, Italian, Hungarian and Spanish) wants to be the landing point for a new concept of prices, security and functionality: Not only do we offer Hosting House but Themes for WordPress and Joomla, Cyber Security services, migration from simple website to the most complex eCommerce. We also offer CRM and ERP solutions developed directly by us and hosted on our servers always in Backup.

Exceptional service is at the heart of everything we do.
We take information security and management seriously. We are ISO 27001 accredited by the British Assessment Bureau.We work with organizations from many different industries, partnering with them as an extension of their team. We build relationships that last and support them as they grow. Our clients rely on us to build bespoke business solutions.
